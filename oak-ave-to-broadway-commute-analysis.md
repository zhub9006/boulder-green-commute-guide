## Seasonal Considerations

### Spring–Fall (March–November) ✅
- Ideal for both walking and biking
- Mild temperatures and long daylight hours
- Watch for afternoon thunderstorms (June–August)
- Bring layers for temperature variability

### Winter (December–February) ❄️
- **Walking:** Feasible with proper footwear and layered clothing
- **Biking:** Requires winter gear:
  - Studded tires or fat bike for snow/ice
  - Fenders for wet roads
  - Front and rear lights (short daylight hours)
  - Waterproof outerwear
- Highway 119 and major routes are typically plowed promptly
- Side streets may remain icy — consider bus-bike combo on worst days
