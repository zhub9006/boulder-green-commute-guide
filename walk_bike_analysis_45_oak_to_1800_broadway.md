### Walking Safety Tips ⚠️
- Oak Avenue and 19th Street have good sidewalks for most of the route
- **Caution at Arapahoe Avenue crossing** — it's a major road with moderate traffic
- Pearl Street Mall is completely car-free — the safest part of the walk
- **Winter recommendation:** 19th Street can be icy — consider carb spikes or microspikes in winter
- Time your walk to avoid the 4:30–5:30 PM rush on 19th Street

## 💡 Pro Tips for This Route

1. **Biking time-saver:** Use the 15th/17th Street bike route on the east side to avoid the Pearl Street Mall pedestrian zone
2. **Walking enjoyment:** The Canyon Blvd stretch along Boulder Creek is one of the most beautiful urban paths in Colorado — take it slow and enjoy!
3. **Weather prep:** Boulder weather is unpredictable — carry a lightweight rain jacket year-round
4. **Music/podcast:** 48 minutes of walking = 3–4 podcast episodes; 15 minutes of biking = 1 long-form episode
5. **Buddy system:** Find a walking/biking partner — it's safer and more fun
6. **Shower facilities:** Check if your office has a shower — biking works best when you can freshen up
7. **B-Cycle:** If your phone dies or you're late, the B-Cycle station at 1800 Broadway can save you
