## Key Intersections

1. **Oak Avenue & 19th Street** — Primary turn from residential to arterial road
2. **19th Street & 20th St.** — Transition point; continues south
3. **20th Street & Pearl Street** — Enter historic downtown district
4. **Pearl Street & 18th Street** — Downtown core
5. **18th Street & Canyon Boulevard** — Final turn toward Broadway
6. **Canyon Boulevard & Broadway** — Destination area (1800 Broadway)

## Route Characteristics

- **Elevation Change:** Slight downhill from north Boulder to downtown (net loss ~20–30 m)
- **Road Type:** Mix of residential streets and urban arterials
- **Bike Infrastructure:** Protected bike lanes on portions of 19th Street; shared lanes downtown
- **Sidewalk Availability:** Continuous sidewalks along entire route
- **Lighting:** Street lights on 19th Street and downtown; limited on some residential segments

## Alternative Routes

### Via Boulder Creek Path (Longer but Scenic)
- Follow Boulder Creek Path from north Boulder south through the canyon
- Adds ~1–2 km but offers river views and reduced traffic
- Best for weekend recreational rides

### Via Arapahoe Avenue (Fewer Stops)
- Take Arapahoe east, then south to Broadway
- More intersections but less dense downtown traffic
- Suitable for biking when 19th Street is congested
