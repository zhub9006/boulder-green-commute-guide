### Enviromental Features
- **Altitude:** 5,430 ft — low oxygen, increased UV, dehydration risk. Drink more water!
- **Sun:** High altitude = stronger UV ray exposure; SPF 30+ recommended for walking/biking
- **Wind:** Boulder is known as the " Windswept Flatiron" city — Canyon Blvd can be gusty
- **Air quality:** Generally excellent in Boulder; wildfire smoke in summer/fall can temporarily degrade it
- **Seasonal wildlife:** Boulder Creek herons, squirrels, raptors in open space
