## 🚧 Alternative & Hybrid Routes

### Hybrid Option: Bike + RTD Bus (Bad Weather Days)

If weather isn't cooperating, combine biking with Boulder's RTD bus system:

1. **Bike** from Oak Avenue to the **19th & B. it Station** (0.5 km — already on route)
2. **Board Bus Line 3** (19th Street corridor) heading Boulder Transit Center
3. **Get off** at **18th & Pearl Station** (2 stops, ~3 min)
4. **Walk** 150 m east to 1800 Broadway

This hybrid approach handles rain, snow, or extreme heat while still getting exercise on each end.

### Alternates During Winter

- **Route A:** Same streets but use Boulder Creek Path (safer, plowed) — adds 200 m
- **Route B:** Use Folsom Street bike route (less traffic, better salted) — adds 300 m  
- **Route C:** Stay on 19th Street the entire way (better plowed) — adds 5 min
