# Green Commuting Tips & Seasonal Guide

## Spring: Mild & wildflowers! Rain jacket for PM storms.
## Summer: VERY hot at elevation! Hydrate 1L+. SPF 30+. UV 25% stronger.
## Fall: BEST! Cool, aspens gold on 19th St. Perfect biking weather.
## Winter: Ice is #1 hazard. Studded tires. Headlight + reflector REQUIRED. Canyon Blvd better maintained.

## Gear: Trail runners, rain jacket, headlamp(winter). Helmet(legal). Fenders(slush). U-lock+t-light.
## Apps: Strava, B-Cycle App, RTD App, Ride with GPS.
## Habits: Reusable bottle. Pack lunch. Belt-drive bike. Bike for groceries. Support local cafés.

## Resources: bikeboulder.org, bouldercolorado.gov/openspace, ecocycle.org
