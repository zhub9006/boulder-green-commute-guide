## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### Walking Route (~48 minutes)

1. **Head south on Oak Avenue** — 97 m — Quiet residential road with sidewalk and Elm canopy. Cross 19th Street intersection carefully.
2. **Continue south on 19th Street** — 2,157 m — Main stretch (~3.5 min walking). Tree-lined arterial with RTD bus routes 1/3/7/10. Use crosswalks at intersections.
3. **Turn right onto 20th Street** — 106 m — Brief jog west. Connect 19th to Pearl St corridor.
4. **Continue south on 20th Street** — 566 m — Tree-lined residential stretch, low traffic. Continue toward Pearl St.
5. **Turn left onto Pearl Street** — 216 m — Historic Pearl Street Mall area. Dismount and walk bike in crowded sections. Bike racks nearby.
6. **Turn left onto 18th Street** — 249 m — Short connector heading south toward Canyon Blvd.
7. **Continue onto Canyon Boulevard** — 637 m — **Boulder Creek Path** — the highlight! Dedicated multi-use path alongside Boulder Creek with Flatirons views.

### Biking Route (~16 minutes)

1. **Head south on Oak Avenue** — 97 m — Very quiet street; easy start with no bike infrastructure needed.
2. **Continue on 19th Street** — 2,157 m — Main stretch (~3 min). Use painted bike lane; listen for RTD buses at intersections. Longest segment.
3. **Turn right onto 20th Street** — 106 m — Quick connector; moderate car traffic.
4. **Continue south on 20th Street** — 566 m — Continue to Pearl St. Bike lane or shared path.
5. **Turn left onto Pearl Street** — 216 m — Dismount in pedestrian-heavy areas. Watch for tourists and shoppers.
6. **Turn left onto 18th Street** — 249 m — Brief segment; moderate bike traffic.
7. **Take Canyon Boulevard** — 637 m — **Speed down the dedicated multi-use path!** Safest and most scenic segment. ⭐ Separated bike lane ⭐
