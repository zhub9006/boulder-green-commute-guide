## Decision Matrix

### Choose 🚶 **Walking** if you:
- Want maximum health benefits from your commute
- Have 48+ minutes per commute
- Prefer zero environmental impact
- Enjoy fresh air and outdoor exercise
- Don't need to carry heavy items

### Choose 🚲 **Biking** if you:
- Want a faster commute (~16 min vs 48 min)
- Need some exercise but want it quicker
- Want to arrive fresh and not sweaty
- Have fair weather most of the time
- Want a good middle ground

### Choose 🚌 **Bus** if you:
- Need a backup plan for bad weather
- Want a predictable commute time
- Don't want to deal with parking
- Have cargo or packages to carry

## Combined Strategies

| Combo | How It Works | Benefit |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| Walk + Bike | Bike 2–3 days, walk 2 days | Flexibility + exercise variety |
| Bike + Bus | Bike to bus stop, bus partway | Fastest with less exertion |
| Walk + Bus | Walk to bus stop, bus downtown | Minimal exertion on transit days |
