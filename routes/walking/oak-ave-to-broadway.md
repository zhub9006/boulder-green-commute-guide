# 🚶 Walking Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

## Route Summary

| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Total Distance** | 4,026 m (2.5 miles) |
| **Estimated Time** | ~48 minutes at comfortable pace (5 km/h) |
| **Terrain** | Predominantly flat; slight elevation near Canyon Boulevard |
| **Sidewalk Quality** | Good along 19th Street; acceptable on side streets |

---

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### Segment 1: Oak Avenue
- **Distance:** 97 m
- **Time:** ~1 min
- **Direction:** Head south on Oak Avenue from your front door
- **Notes:** Short residential stretch. Watch for pedestrians and children.

### Segment 2: 19th Street (Southbound)
- **Distance:** 2,157 m (the longest segment)
- **Time:** ~3 min 30 sec
- **Direction:** Continue south on 19th Street
- **Notes:** Major north-south corridor with sidewalks. Moderate vehicle traffic. This is the backbone of the route.

### Segment 3: 20th Street (Connector)
- **Distance:** 106 m
- **Time:** ~12 sec
- **Direction:** Brief connector between 19th and 20th
- **Notes:** Quick turn; stay alert for turning vehicles.

### Segment 4: 20th Street (Southbound)
- **Distance:** 566 m
- **Time:** ~55 sec
- **Direction:** Continue south on 20th Street
- **Notes:** Less traffic than 19th. Residential feel.

### Segment 5: Pearl Street (Westbound)
- **Distance:** 216 m
- **Time:** ~25 sec
- **Direction:** Turn west onto Pearl Street
- **Notes:** Classic Boulder thoroughfare. The Pearl Street Mall is a pedestrian-friendly zone.

### Segment 6: 18th Street (Southbound)
- **Distance:** 249 m
- **Time:** ~39 sec
- **Direction:** Turn south onto 18th Street
- **Notes:** Short stretch leading toward Canyon Boulevard.

### Segment 7: Canyon Boulevard (Westbound)
- **Distance:** 637 m
- **Time:** ~1 min 9 sec
- **Direction:** Turn west onto Canyon Boulevard
- **Notes:** Part of the Boulder Creek Path — a paved multi-use trail alongside Boulder Creek. Beautiful and scenic!

## Seasonal Considerations

| Season | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Spring** | Pleasant temps; watch for muddy sections after rain |

## Accessibility
- **Wheelchair accessible:** Mostly yes, but some corners have steep curb ramps
- **Stroller friendly:** Good on 19th and 20th Streets; Canyon Boulevard path is smooth
- **Dog-friendly:** Plenty of green space along the route; bring bags!
