## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### Segment 1: Oak Avenue (97 m / 16 sec)
1. Exit 45 Oak Avenue and head **south**
2. Follow the sidewalk along Oak Avenue
3. Continue for approximately **100 meters**
4. Turn right onto **19th Street** at the intersection

> 🏠 *You'll pass mature cottonwood trees and residential Boulder homes.*

### Segment 2: 19th Street (2.16 km / ~3.5 min)
5. Turn left onto **19th Street** and head **south**
6. Continue along 19th Street for **1.6 km** — this is a major north-south corridor with sidewalks
7. Pass through the **Hillview** neighborhood
8. Continue past **Village Center** shopping area (on your left at ~1.1 km)
9. Cross over **Arapahoe Avenue** (main east-west arterial)
10. Continue south on 19th Street for another **600 meters**
11. Cross **Baseline Road** — major intersection with signals

> ⚠️ *Use crosswalks — 19th Street is busy during rush hour. Sidewalks are well-maintained but watch for opening car doors.*

### Segment 3: 20th Street Connection (106 m / 12 sec)
12. Turn right briefly onto **20th Street**
13. Connected short jog — approximately **100 meters**

### Segment 4: 20th Street (566 m / ~1 min)
14. Turn left onto **20th Street** heading **south**
15. Continue for **566 meters** with sidewalk on both sides

### Segment 5: Pearl Street (216 m / 26 sec)
16. Turn right onto **Pearl Street** heading **west**
17. Cross short distance through downtown — **Pedestrian Mall zone**
18. Enjoy the murals and street performers!

> 🎭 *Pearl Street is Boulder's vibrant pedestrian mall — take a moment to enjoy the atmosphere.*

### Segment 6: 18th Street (249 m / 37 sec)
19. Turn left onto **18th Street** heading **south**
20. Continue for **249 meters**

### Segment 7: Canyon Boulevard (637 m / ~1 min)
21. Continue onto **Canyon Boulevard** — Boulder's iconic multi-use path
22. This path is **completely separated from vehicle traffic**
23. Enjoy views of the Flatirons to your left
24. Continue south along Canyon Boulevard for **637 meters**

### Arrival: 1800 Broadway (0 m)
25. Turn left onto **Broadway** at the signal light
26. 1800 Broadway is on your **right** — modern office complex

> 🏢 *Look for the blue-green glass office building with the landscaped plaza.*

- ⚠️ **Baseline Rd crossing:** High traffic volume — use signal crosswalks

- ✅ **Pearl St section:** Pedestrian-priority zone — take your time
