## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### Segment 1: Oak Avenue (97 m)
1. Exit 45 Oak Avenue heading **south**
2. Use the **bike lane** or road (low traffic)
3. Continue for **~100 meters**
4. Turn right onto **19th Street** bike lane

> 🛡️ *Oak Avenue has low volume and a designated bike lane.*

### Segment 2: 19th Street (2.16 km)
5. Follow the **green-painted bike lane** south on **19th Street**
6. This is Boulder's **primary north-south bike corridor**
7. Pass through Hillview neighborhood — bike lanes are well-maintained
8. At ~1.6 km, you'll pass **Boulder High School** — slow down, watch for students
9. Cross **Arapahoe Avenue** using bike signal (dedicated bike traffic light)
10. Continue 600m to **Baseline Road**

> 🚦 *19th St. bike lanes are signal-protected at major intersections. Use them!*

### Segment 3: 20th Street Brief (106 m)
11. Brief jog right onto **20th Street** — secure, low-traffic connector

### Segment 4: 20th Street (566 m)
12. Left onto **20th Street** — another **separated bike lane**
13. Continue south for **566 meters**

### Segment 5: Pearl Street (216 m)
14. Right onto **Pearl Street** — **bike-pedestrian shared path**
15. **Dismount** in the pedestrian zone (walk your bike)
16. This section is **car-free** — relax and enjoy!

> ⚠️ *Walk your bike on Pearl Street pedestrian mall. It's the law and courteous to pedestrians.*

### Segment 6: 18th Street (249 m)
17. Right onto **18th Street** — bike lane available
18. Head south for **249 meters**

### Segment 7: Canyon Boulevard (637 m) 🌟
19. Merge onto **Canyon Boulevard Multi-Use Path** — **Boulder's crown jewel**
20. Wide, **completely car-free** path with beautiful scenery
21. Ride along the base of the Flatirons
22. Enjoy the breeze and views!
23. Continue for **637 meters**

### Arrival: 1800 Broadway
24. Exit Canyon Boulevard path at Broadway
25. Use the **bike signal** to cross Broadway safely
26. 1800 Broadway bike racks are immediately on your **left**

> 🔒 **Secure your bike:** Multiple U-lock racks available at building entrances.

## Safety & Tips

- ✅ **Night riding:** All bike lanes have lighting; Canyon Blvd path is well-lit
- ✅ **Winter riding:** Canyon Blvd path isPriority-plowed; 19th St. lanes plowed within 24h
- ⚠️ **Afternoon wind:** Prevailing westerlies can be strong on Canyon Blvd — lower gears help
- ⚠️ **Dismount zones:** Pearl Street and some 19th St. intersections require walking your bike
