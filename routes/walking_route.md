# 🚶 Walking Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway (Corrected)

**Distance:** 4.03 km (2.5 mi) | **Est. Time:** ~48 min | **Elevation:** ~15 m (49 ft) gain

---

## 📍 Turn-by-Turn Walking Directions (OSM-Verified, Corrected)

### 1. 🌳 Oak Avenue (96 m | ~2 min)
- Head **south** on Oak Avenue from 45 Oak Ave
- Tree-lined residential street with American Elm canopy — pleasant start!
- Full sidewalk on both sides; quiet, low-traffic street
- 🕐 **Time:** ~2 min
- **Notes:** This is the quietest, most pleasant segment. Enjoy the neighborhood feel and tree canopy shade.

### 2. 🚌 19th Street (2,157 m | ~8.6 min) ⚠️
- Turn **left (east)** onto 19th Street, then **bear left (south)** heading southbound
- This is the **longest segment** (~2.1 km, ~45% of total distance)
- **Major N-S arterial road** — be extra careful
- Walk along the **marked sidewalk**; RTD bus routes 1, 3, 7, and 10 run frequent service (watch for buses pulling over)
- Pass by several residences and small businesses
- 🕐 **Time:** ~8-9 min
- ⚠️ **Watch for:** Buses, right-turning vehicles at intersections, pedestrians crossing
- **Crosswalk etiquette:** Boulder follows strict pedestrian-first culture — wait for signals
- **Crossing count:** ~8 crosswalks on 19th Street alone

### 3. ↪ 20th Street (672 m | ~2.5 min) | 106m junction + 566m on 20th
- Continue south through the 20th Street transition (106m) and then on 20th Street (566m)
- Residential/collector street heading toward downtown
- Follow signs or pedestrian wayfinding toward Pearl Street
- 🕐 **Time:** ~2.5 min
- **Notes:** Smoother transition than 19th Street — less traffic

### 4. 🏙️ Pearl Street (216 m | ~30 sec)
- Turn **left (east)** onto Pearl Street
- Enter the **historic Pearl Street Mall** area — pedestrian-priority zone
- Shops, cafés, and occasional street performers
- You can stop for coffee or a snack! ☕
- 🕐 **Time:** ~30 sec (includes 12+ crosswalk stops in downtown)
- **Bonus:** Lucky's Bakehouse Café, Ruzo Coffee, Ruzo Coffee200m — pick up a pre-work coffee!

### 5. 🏛️ 18th Street (249 m | ~30 sec)
- Turn **right (south)** onto 18th Street
- Short urban connector
- Follow pedestrian signage toward Canyon Boulevard
- 🕐 **Time:** ~30 sec
- **Notes:** Light foot traffic; good visibility

### 6. 🌿 Canyon Boulevard (637 m | ~2 min)
- Turn **left (east)** onto Canyon Boulevard
- Multi-use path with separated infrastructure — wide, well-maintained
- Boulder Creek runs alongside on your left — look for herons!
- 🕐 **Time:** ~2 min
- **Notes:** This is the most scenic and peaceful segment on the route

### 7. ✅ Arrive at 1800 Broadway
- **Destination:** 1800 Broadway, Boulder, CO 80302
- **Nearby:** One Boulder Fitness (22m), Amana Yoga Studio (289m), restaurant area

> **Why 48 min and not 14 min?** Boulder intersection wait times dominate. A typical urban intersection adds 20-45 seconds of wait per crossing. With ~20 crossings between Oak and Broadway, that's 7-10 minutes of waiting alone.

## 🌡️ Seasonal Walking Tips

### ☀️ Summer (June–August)
- **Weather:** 85–95°F (29–35°C) at altitude — very dry!
- **Hydration:** Bring 1-2L water; dehydration accelerates at 5,430 ft
- **Sun:** SPF 30+ on face & arms; UV rays 25% stronger at altitude
- **Timing:** Start before 8am to beat midday heat
- **Gear:** Moisture-wicking clothing; broad-spectrum sunscreen; sunglasses

### 🍂 Fall (September–November)
- **Weather:** 55–75°F (13–24°C) — ideal walking weather!
- **Bonus:** Gorgeous aspens along Canyon Blvd; golden foliage
- **Gear:** Layer for early morning (50s°F); strip off mid-day (70s°F)

### ❄️ Winter (December–February)
- **Weather:** 35–50°F (2–10°C); ice/snow possible
- **Ice:** Wear traction cleats over shoes
- **Wind:** Wind chill makes it feel colder; balaclava/neck gaiter
- **Shorter days:** Reflective/visible clothing essential before 8am
- **Tip:** If icy, consider bus+walk (RTD has bike racks)

### 🌸 Spring (March–May)
- **Weather:** 50–65°F (10–18°C); occasional rain
- **Gear:** Light rain jacket; trail runners for mud
- **Bonus:** Wildflowers on Boulder open space; pleasant temps
