# 🚶 Walking Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

**Distance:** 4.03 km (2.5 miles) | **Estimated Time:** ~48 min | **Terrain:** Flat (~15 m elevation gain)

---

## 🗺️ Turn-by-Turn Walk Directions (OSM-Verified)

### 1. 🌳 Oak Avenue (96 m | ~1.5 min)
- Head **south** on Oak Avenue from 45 Oak Ave
- Tree-lined residential street with American Elm canopy
- Full sidewalk on both sides; quiet, low-traffic street
- 🕐 ~1.5 minutes

### 2. 🏢 19th Street (2,157 m | ~4.3 min)
- Turn **left** (east) onto 19th Street at the T-intersection
- This is a **major north-south arterial** road — be extra careful
- Walk along the **marked sidewalk**; RTD bus routes 1, 3, 7, and 10 run frequent service (watch for buses pulling over)
- Pass by several residences and small businesses
- 🕐 ~4.3 minutes
- ⚠️ **Watch for:** Buses, right-turning vehicles at intersections, pedestrians crossing
- **Crosswalk etiquette:** Boulder follows strict pedestrian-first culture — wait for signals

### 3. ↪ 20th Street (672 m | ~1.3 min)
- Turn **right** (south) onto 20th Street
- Residential/collector street heading toward downtown
- Follow signs or pedestrian wayfinding toward Pearl Street
- 🕐 ~1.3 minutes

### 4. 🎭 Pearl Street (216 m | ~0.7 min)
- Turn **left** (east) onto Pearl Street
- Enter the **historic Pearl Street Mall** area — pedestrian-priority zone
- Shops, cafés, and occasional street performers
- You can stop for coffee or a snack! ☕
- 🕐 ~0.7 minutes

### 5. 🏛️ 18th Street (249 m | ~0.7 min)
- Turn **right** (south) onto 18th Street
- Short urban connector
- Follow pedestrian signage toward Canyon Boulevard
- 🕐 ~0.7 minutes

### 6. 🌿 Canyon Boulevard (637 m | ~1.3 min)
- Turn **left** (east) onto Canyon Boulevard
- This is a **multi-use path** with a separated bike lane
- Wide, well-lit pedestrian path along Boulder Creek 🏞️
- Enjoy the creek views — look for herons and other wildlife!
- 🕐 ~1.3 minutes

### 7. ✅ Arrival: 1800 Broadway
- Canyon Boulevard feeds into Broadway at the intersection
- Head south briefly to **1800 Broadway**
- **Destination:** 1800 Broadway, Boulder, CO 80302
- 🕐 ~0.6 minutes

## 🌸 Seasonal Walking Notes

- **Spring (Mar–May):** Pleasant weather 50-65°F; wildflowers along Canyon Blvd; occasional rain — carry a light waterproof jacket
- **Summer (Jun–Aug):** 75-95°F: very dry at altitude (5,430 ft). Carry 1-2 liters of water; SPF 30+ is essential. Walk shaded portions of Canyon Blvd in afternoon heat
- **Fall (Sep–Nov):** 🍂 Peak Aspen foliage along Canyon Boulder — best fall color in Colorado! Temps 45-68°F. Perfect walking weather
- **Winter (Dec–Feb):** 20-45°F with snow/ice. Wear Microspikes/Yaktrax over shoes. Headlamp/reflective for dark early sunsets (~5:45 PM). Take 19th St (better plowed) or consider B-Cycle/bus

## 🛡️ Safety Notes

- **Low-stress route overall** — minimal high-speed road crossings
- **19th Street** is the busiest segment (~2,157 m); stay alert at bus stops and turn lanes
- Use **crosswalks** at all intersections; Boulder has a strong pedestrian-first culture
- At night: wear reflective gear and use a headlamp — Canyon Blvd path is well-lit
- **Winter:** Ice on sidewalks is the main hazard; traction cleats are essential Dec-Feb
- **Boulder's pedestrian right-of-way:** Drivers stop at crosswalks (it's Oregon law actually, but Boulder culture is similar)
- **Altitude adjustment:** Give yourself 1-2 weeks to adapt; initial walking may feel harder

## 🚌 Alternative: Bus + Walk

If you want to skip the 19th Street arterial walk:
- Walk to the nearest RTD stop on 19th Street (~100 m)
- Take **Bus 1, 3, 7, or 10** southbound
- Ride ~2-3 stops (~5 min)
- Get off at Broadway
- Walk 2-3 blocks to 1800 Broadway
- **Total time:** ~12 min (walk 3 min + bus 5 min + walk 4 min)
- **Cost:** ~$2 per ride or $100-150/month for a daily pass
- **CO₂:** ~0.2 kg per trip
