# 🚶‍♂️ Walking Route: Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

## Overview
- **Distance:** 4.0 km (2.5 miles)
- **Time:** ~48 minutes
- **Calories Burned:** ~220 calories
- **Difficulty:** Easy — Flat terrain, well-maintained sidewalks

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

1. **Head south on Oak Avenue** (97 m, ~2 min)
   - Start at 45 Oak Avenue
   - Walk south toward 19th Street intersection

2. **Turn left onto 19th Street** (2.2 km, ~27 min)
   - Main stretch of the walk
   - Sidewalk available on both sides
   - Pass by residential properties and local shops
   - Continue south through the neighborhood

3. **Turn right onto 20th St.** (106 m, ~1 min)
   - Short connector between 19th and 20th Streets

4. **Continue east on 20th Street** (566 m, ~7 min)
   - Cross through downtown area
   - Watch for pedestrian crossings
   - Pass by local businesses

5. **Turn right onto Pearl Street** (216 m, ~3 min)
   - Historic Pearl Street Mall area
   - Brick pavers — watch your step!
   - Tourists and locals alike

6. **Turn left onto 18th Street** (249 m, ~3 min)
   - Head toward Canyon Boulevard
   - Quieter street with good pedestrian access

7. **Turn right onto Canyon Boulevard** (637 m, ~8 min)
   - Scenic walk along Boulder Creek
   - Dedicated pedestrian path
   - Natural beauty — watch for wildlife!

8. **Arrive at 1800 Broadway** — your destination on the left

## Safety Tips
- Use crosswalks at all intersections
- Be alert for cyclists on shared paths
- Carry water — it's a Boulder desert climate
- Morning walks: dress in layers, temps can be 10-15°F cooler
- Evening walks: use reflective gear and carry a light

## Alternatives
- **Faster route:** Same path but take 20th Street all the way through without the Pearl St detour (saves ~3 min)
- **Scenic route:** Take the Boulder Creek Path from near 20th Street for a more scenic approach via Canyon Boulevard
- **Weather backup:** Consider the RTD bus route 200 or 400 — bikes welcome if you mixed modes
