# Walking Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

## Turn-by-Turn Walking Directions

### Step 1: Depart — Oak Avenue (96.6 m)
- Exit 45 Oak Avenue heading south on the sidewalk
- Walk under mature American Elm trees — quiet and shaded
- **~1 minute**

### Step 2: Turn right — 19th Street (2,156.5 m)
- Turn **right** (south) onto **19th Street**
- Longest segment — 2.2 km heading south
- Use wide sidewalk on both sides
- Cross **Euclid Avenue** (signalized crosswalk)
- Pass **CU Boulder campus area** — see students and green spaces
- Cross **21st Street** and **23rd Street**
- You will pass local businesses and cafés
- **~8 minutes**

### Step 3: Connectors — 20th St. & 20th Street (671.7 m)
- Turn **right** (west) onto **20th St.** — short connector
- Continue **south** on **20th Street** — residential, tree-lined
- Watch for the pedestrian crossing at **Pearl Street**
- **~3 minutes**

### Step 4: Turn left — Pearl Street (216.2 m)
- Turn **left** (east) onto **Pearl Street**
- Near the historic **Pearl Street Mall**
- Great place to grab a coffee or snack before heading to work
- **~1 minute**

### Step 5: Turn right — 18th Street (248.8 m)
- Turn **right** (north) onto **18th Street**
- Short but busy section near downtown
- Watch for pedestrians
- **~1 minute**

### Step 6: Turn left — Canyon Boulevard (636.6 m)
- Turn **left** (west) onto **Canyon Boulevard**
- Dedicated separated pedestrian path
- **Flatirons views** open up as you walk
- Wide, well-maintained, separated from car traffic
- Most pleasant and safest final segment
- **~2 minutes**

### Step 7: Arrive — 1800 Broadway
- Walk west along Canyon Blvd toward Broadway
- Arrive at **1800 Broadway** on your right
- **Destination reached!**

## Seasonal Walking Tips

**Spring (Mar–May):** Temps 10–20°C — perfect walking weather. Trail flowers bloom along Canyon Blvd. Bring a light jacket.

**Summer (Jun–Aug):** Temps 25–35°C — hot! Walk early morning or evening. Hydrate well. Tree canopy on Oak Ave provides shade. Canyon Blvd has a cooling breeze near the creek.

**Fall (Sep–Nov):** Temps 5–20°C — ideal season. Aspen leaves turn golden along Canyon Blvd. Less rain than spring.

**Winter (Dec–Feb):** Temps -5–5°C — cold, possible snow/ice. Wear warm layers, waterproof boots, reflective gear. 19th St sidewalks cleared by city; Canyon Blvd path plowed. Start earlier to avoid dark walks.

## Safety Tips for Walkers

1. **Cross at signalized intersections** — 19th St at Euclid Ave and 19th St at 21st St have pedestrian signals.
2. **Stay on sidewalks** — especially on 19th St where some sections have construction.
3. **Watch for bikes** on Canyon Blvd shared path — cyclists go faster.
4. **Night walking** — use a headlamp or reflective vest. Oak Ave and residential streets are unlit.
5. **Dogs** — route passes through residential areas with dogs; give space.
