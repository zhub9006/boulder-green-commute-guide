## FAQ

**Q: Is this route safe for solo commuters?**  
A: Yes. Boulder is one of Colorado's most bike/ped-friendly cities. The final Canyon Boulevard segment is particularly well-lit and populated.

**Q: Can I walk in business casual?**  
A: Absolutely — plan a 5-minute buffer to freshen up at building restrooms. 48 minutes is leisurely enough to arrive without perspiring heavily.

**Q: How about rain/snow?**  
A: Waterproof outer layers and shoe covers transform this commute in wet weather. The Canyon Boulevard path drains well.

**Q: Is e-bike worth it?**  
A: Definitely — 12 minutes vs. 16 minutes (bike) or 48 minutes (walk), while still being zero-emission at the point of travel. Consider a pedal-assist for hilly alternatives on other routes.

**Q: What about mosquitoes in summer?**  
A: Boulder Creek can have mosquitoes June–August. A light long-sleeve layer or DEET spray keeps them away.
