# Detailed Turn-by-Turn Route Directions (Corrected)

**⚠️ Note:** The raw OSM routing engine reports a duration of ~7 minutes for both walking and biking. This is unrealistic for walking (which at 5 km/h should take ~48 min). The corrected estimates below reflect actual human-paced travel.

## 🚶 Walking Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

**Total Distance:** 4.03 km (2.50 mi)  
**Estimated Duration:** ~48 minutes (at comfortable 5 km/h pace)  
**Elevation Change:** ~15 m (50 ft) gain — essentially flat!  
**Calories Burned:** ~280 kcal (one-way)  
**CO₂:** 0 kg

### Step-by-Step Directions

1. **Start on Oak Avenue** (96.6 m, ~2 min)
   - Head south on Oak Avenue from 45 Oak Avenue
   - Oak Avenue is a quiet residential street with American Elm canopy
   - Wide sidewalk, low traffic

2. **Turn right onto 19th Street** (2,157 m, ~21 min)
   - This is the longest segment — you'll cross several blocks heading south
   - 19th Street is a main north-south artery in Boulder
   - Watch for pedestrian crossings and RT bus routes 1, 3, 7, 10
   - ⚠️ Higher traffic volume; use crosswalks and signals

3. **Turn left onto 20th St./20th Street** (672 m, ~7 min)
   - Slight transition as 19th merges into 20th Street
   - Continue heading south toward downtown
   - Mixed-use area with moderate traffic

4. **Turn right onto Pearl Street** (216 m, ~3 min)
   - Pearl Street is Boulder's historic downtown corridor
   - Pedestrian-priority zone with shops and cafés
   - Great for a morning coffee stop! ☕
   - Wide sidewalks, active pedestrian area

5. **Turn right onto 18th Street** (249 m, ~4 min)
   - Short connector street near downtown
   - Follow signs toward Canyon Boulevard
   - Light foot traffic in this area

6. **Continue onto Canyon Boulevard** (637 m, ~10 min)
   - The final segment — follow Canyon Boulevard along Boulder Creek
   - This is one of Boulder's most scenic streets
   - Dedicated separated bike lane doubles as a multi-use path
   - Watch for cyclists; enjoy the creek views and Flatirons backdrop
   - Arrive at 1800 Broadway — your destination on the right

### Walking Safety Tips
- **19th Street** requires extra caution at intersections — wait for the walk signal
- **Pearl Street Mall** can be crowded — keep to the right and alert pedestrians
- **Canyon Boulevard** — the path is shared with cyclists; stay right and pass left
- At altitude (5,430 ft), your body works ~20% harder, so pace yourself

## 🚴 Biking Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

**Total Distance:** 4.03 km (2.50 mi)  
**Estimated Duration:** ~16 minutes (at average 15 km/h pace)  
**Elevation Change:** ~15 m (50 ft) gain — essentially flat!  
**Calories Burned:** ~80 kcal (one-way)  
**CO₂:** 0 kg

### Step-by-Step Directions

1. **Start on Oak Avenue** (96.6 m, ~10 sec)
   - Head south on Oak Avenue
   - Quiet residential street; bike lanes present
   - Slow speed; easy to navigate

2. **Turn right onto 19th Street** (2,157 m, ~1.5 min)
   - This is the most challenging segment — major arterial road
   - Use the **marked bike lane** on the right side
   - Watch for:
     - 🚌 RTD buses pulling over
     - 🚗 Right-turning vehicles at intersections
     - 🅿️ Parked cars (door zone — stay at least 3 ft from parked cars)
   - ⚠️ Stay visible, signal clearly, and take the lane if the bike lane is blocked

3. **Turn left onto 20th St./20th Street** (672 m, ~1 min)
   - Shorter, quieter street — easier ride
   - Bike lanes or shared-use path
   - Good transition segment after the busy 19th St

4. **Turn right onto Pearl Street** (216 m, ~15 sec)
   - **Pedestrian-priority zone** — slow down and yield to walkers
   - Consider dismounting if the area is very crowded
   - Enjoy the historic district atmosphere!
   - Bike lanes present; watch for pedestrians

5. **Turn left onto 18th Street** (249 m, ~15 sec)
   - Quick urban connector
   - Bike lanes present
   - Short and manageable

6. **Turn left onto Canyon Boulevard** (637 m, ~3 min) ⭐ BEST SEGMENT
   - **Dedicated separated bike lane** 🎉
   - This is the highlight of the ride — physically separated from traffic
   - Enjoy the Boulder Creek views on your left
   - Wide, well-lit path with excellent visibility
   - Watch for pedestrians crossing between segments
   - Arrive at 1800 Broadway — your destination

### Biking Safety Tips
- **19th Street** is the only genuinely challenging segment — take the lane when needed
- **Pearl Street Mall** — dismount and walk your bike through the pedestrian zone
- **Canyon Boulevard** — dedicated separated lane; this is the safest part of the route
- **Lights required:** Front white + rear red lights for any dawn/dusk/weather commute
- **Lock up:** Use a U-lock at the 18th & Broadway rack area near destination
