## Safety Notes

1. **19th Street (2.2 km)** is the longest and busiest segment. Stay in the bike lane and watch for right-turning vehicles.
2. **Pearl Street Mall** requires you to dismount your bike and walk through the pedestrian zone.
3. **Canyon Boulevard** is the safest segment — the separated bike lane means minimal car interaction.
4. **Dawn and dusk** — wear reflective gear. The route has streetlights on 19th St and 20th St but Canyon Blvd can be darker.
5. **Winter conditions** — Boulder winter snow can make 19th St slippery. Bike lane may not be cleared first. Consider biking on 20th St instead.
6. **Rain** — the Canyon Blvd separated bike lane drains well and stays relatively dry.
