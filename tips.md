# Sustainable Commuting Tips for Boulder Residents

## 🚲 Biking Best Practices

### Route Planning
- **Canyon Boulevard** is your best friend — it has the most dedicated bike infrastructure in Boulder
- Use the **Boulder Creek Path** for a scenic, low-traffic alternative
- Avoid Pearl Street Mall during peak shopping hours (dismount zone)
- Check for seasonal construction or road closures on [Boulder's city page](https://bouldercolorado.gov)

### Safety
- Always wear a helmet (Colorado law for riders under 18, but smart for all ages)
- Use front and rear lights — especially in winter when days are short
- Signal your turns and make eye contact with drivers
- Lock your bike with a U-lock + cable for maximum security

### Gear
- Invest in a comfortable, well-fitted helmet
- Panniers or a backpack for carrying work items
- Reflective clothing for winter commuting
- Fenders for rainy/snowy Boulder days

## 🚶 Walking Best Practices

### Route Planning
- The **Boulder Creek Path** offers a beautiful walking corridor
- Downtown Boulder is highly walkable — enjoy the boutique shops and cafes
- Use the pedestrian signals at major intersections
- Consider walking to a colleague's office if they're nearby

### Comfort
- Wear supportive, weather-appropriate shoes
- Carry a small daypack with essentials
- Use an umbrella or rain jacket — Boulder weather is unpredictable
- In winter, traction aids (ice cleats) are essential

## 🚌 Multi-Modal Options

### Boulder Transit
- Bus routes cover most major corridors in Boulder
- A monthly pass is affordable and convenient
- Combine with biking for the "last mile" problem

### Car-Share & Vanpool
- **Lyft** and **Uber** for occasional need
- **Boulder Vanpool** programs for regular commutes
- **Colorado Bicycle Club** members sometimes offer car-share

### Remote Work
- If possible, negotiate 1–2 remote days per week
- Even one day reduces your weekly emissions by 20%
- Use the time saved for exercise or family

## 🏢 Workplace Initiatives

### Encourage Your Employer
- Propose bike storage and shower facilities
- Start a carpool board or Slack channel
- Offer incentives for green commuters
- Support flexible scheduling to avoid rush hour

### Track Your Impact
- Use apps like **Oroeco** or **JouleBug** to track carbon savings
- Set monthly goals for green commute days
- Share your progress with colleagues to build momentum
