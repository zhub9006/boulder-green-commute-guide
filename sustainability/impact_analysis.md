## Carbon Savings by Switching from Driving

- **Per one-way trip:** 1.5 kg CO₂ saved
- **Per round trip:** 3.0 kg CO₂ saved
- **Per work week:** 7.5 kg CO₂ saved
- **Per year (250 workdays):** 375 kg CO₂ saved
- **Equivalent:** ~3.75 metric tons of CO₂ over 10 years

## Health Benefits Monetized

- **Walking:** ~480 kcal/day round-trip ≈ $400–$600/year in health savings
- **Biking:** ~600 kcal/day round-trip ≈ $500–$800/year in health savings
- Reduced medical costs, fewer sick days, better productivity

## Water & Pollution Savings

- **Oil/gasoline:** 125 liters (~33 gallons) per year not burned
- **Particulate matter:** Zero particulates from breathing
- **Noise pollution:** Near-silent biking/walking reduces urban noise

## Water Footprint

- Driving requires ~3x more water per mile (refining, cooling, washing)
- Switching saves an estimated ~150,000 liters of embodied water per year
