## Step-by-Step Directions

### Leg 1: Oak Avenue (96.6 m, ~1 min)
Head south on **Oak Avenue** from 45 Oak Avenue. This is your starting segment — the road runs directly south from your residence toward the downtown grid. The residential street has sidewalks and tree canopies.

### Leg 2: 19th Street (2,156.5 m, ~3 min 29 sec)
Turn right onto **19th Street** and head east. This is the longest segment of your walk at over 2 km. 19th Street runs through residential neighborhoods with sidewalks, tree canopies, and low traffic. You'll pass several blocks of housing and cross a few side streets. This is the most scenic part of the route — enjoy the views!

### Leg 3: 20th St. (106.1 m, ~13 sec)
Turn left at the intersection with **20th Street**. This is a short connector segment to align you with the Pearl Street corridor. Quick intersection — stay alert for pedestrians.

### Leg 4: 20th Street (565.6 m, ~1 min 20 sec)
Continue on **20th Street** heading east. Pass through the neighborhood toward downtown. This section has sidewalk infrastructure and relatively low vehicle traffic. You'll pass University Hill campus buildings and student housing.

### Leg 5: Pearl Street (216.2 m, ~26 sec)
Turn onto **Pearl Street**. Pearl Street is a notable Boulder boulevard between downtown and the University Hill neighborhood. Sidewalks are well-maintained here. This is where you'll see the beginning of the famous Pearl Street Mall.

### Leg 6: 18th Street (248.8 m, ~39 sec)
Turn left on **18th Street** heading west. This segment takes you back toward the Broadway corridor. You're now in the heart of downtown Boulder.

### Leg 7: Canyon Boulevard (636.6 m, ~1 min 9 sec)
Turn onto **Canyon Boulevard** and head west. Canyon Boulevard is a major east-west arterial that connects to Broadway. There are dedicated sidewalks along this stretch. As you approach Broadway, you'll see commercial developments and the entrance to your office district.

### Leg 8: Arrival at 1800 Broadway
Continue on Canyon Boulevard until you reach **1800 Broadway**. Your office building is visible from the intersection of Canyon Boulevard and Broadway. 🎉
