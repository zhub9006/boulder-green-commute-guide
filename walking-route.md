- **Hydration:** Bring water, especially in summer at altitude (5,430 ft)
- **Safety:** Use crosswalks; drivers in downtown Boulder are generally bike/walk-aware
- **Enjoy the route:** This walk takes you through some of Boulder's most vibrant neighborhoods
