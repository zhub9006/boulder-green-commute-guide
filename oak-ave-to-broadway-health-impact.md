# Health Impact Analysis: Walking & Biking Commute

> 45 Oak Avenue ↔ 1800 Broadway, Boulder, CO

---

## Walking Health Benefits

### Physical Health

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Weekly Exercise** | Walking 48 min/day × 5 days = **240 min/week** → exceeds CDC's 150 min/week recommendation |
| **Cardiovascular** | Regular walking reduces heart disease risk by ~30% |
| **Weight Management** | Burns ~250 kcal per trip → 125,000 kcal/year = ~17 lbs of fat equivalent |
| **Blood Pressure** | 30 min of moderate walking can lower systolic BP by 5–8 mmHg |
| **Bone Density** | Weight-bearing exercise improves bone health |

### Mental Health

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Stress Reduction** | Outdoor walking reduces cortisol levels by ~15–25% |
| **Mood Improvement** | Endorphin release during moderate exercise |
| **Sleep Quality** | Regular exercise improves sleep onset and duration |
| **Cognitive Function** | Walking increases blood flow to the brain, improving focus and creativity |

---

## Biking Health Benefits

### Physical Health

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Cardiovascular** | Cycling is excellent aerobic exercise; reduces heart disease risk ~40% |
| **Joint Health** | Low-impact — easier on knees and hips than running |
| **Leg Strength** | Builds quadriceps, hamstrings, and calf muscles |
| **Calories Burned** | ~80 kcal per trip → 40,000 kcal/year |
| **Core Stability** | Balancing on a bike engages core muscles |

### Mental Health

| Benefit | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Stress Relief** | Outdoor cycling reduces anxiety and depression symptoms |
| **Sense of Freedom** | Biking provides a feeling of independence and control over commute |
| **Time in Nature** | Exposure to green spaces along the route (Boulder Creek, parks) |

---

## Comparison: Walking vs. Biking

| Health Metric | Walking | Biking |
|--------------|---------|--------|
| **Calories per trip** | ~250 kcal | ~80 kcal |
| **Cardio benefit** | Moderate | High |
| **Joint impact** | Low–moderate | Low |
| **Time efficiency** | Lower (48 min) | Higher (16 min) |
| **Sweat factor** | Moderate | High (may need shower at work) |
| **Consistency** | High (weather permitting) | Moderate (seasonal variation) |

## Annual Health Impact Projection

### Walking 5 days/week × 250 work days:
- **Total exercise:** 1,200 minutes → well above 150 min minimum
- **Calories burned:** 62,500 kcal/year
- **Estimated weight managed:** ~8–10 lbs
- **Reduced sick days:** 1–3 fewer sick days per year
- **Estimated healthcare savings:** $200–$500/year

### Biking 5 days/week × 250 work days:
- **Total exercise:** 480 minutes
- **Calories burned:** 20,000 kcal/year
- **Estimated weight managed:** ~3–5 lbs
- **Reduced sick days:** 1–2 fewer sick days per year
- **Estimated healthcare savings:** $150–$300/year

---

*Sources: CDC Physical Activity Guidelines, American Heart Association, various peer-reviewed studies on active commuting*.
