# 🚶‍♂️ Walking vs. 🚲 Biking: Route Comparison

## Commute: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway, Boulder, CO

| Metric | 🚶 Walking | 🚲 Biking | Winner |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Distance** | 4,026 m (2.5 mi) | 4,026 m (2.5 mi) | Tie |
| **Time (one way)** | ~48 min | ~16 min | 🚲 Biking |
| **Time (round trip)** | ~96 min | ~32 min | 🚲 Biking |
| **Calories burned** | ~240 per trip | ~300 per trip | 🚲 Biking (more burn) |
| **CO₂ saved per trip** | ~1.5 kg | ~1.5 kg | Tie |
| **Weather sensitivity** | High (rain/cold = hard) | Moderate (wind/rain) | 🚲 Biking |
| **Flexibility** | No gear needed | Need bike + lock | 🚶 Walking |
| **Terrain difficulty** | Flat, easy | Flat, easy | Tie |

## Route Segments (Both modes follow the same path)

1. **Oak Avenue** – 97 m, residential street exit
2. **19th Street (southbound)** – 2,157 m, main corridor (~5 min walking / ~1.5 min biking)
3. **20th St. connector** – 106 m, quick turn
4. **20th Street (southbound)** – 566 m, continues toward downtown
5. **Pearl Street (westbound)** – 216 m, enters downtown/Pearl District
6. **18th Street (southbound)** – 249 m, short stretch to creek path
7. **Canyon Boulevard (westbound)** – 637 m, scenic Boulder Creek Path finish

| Scenario | Best Mode | Why |
|---|---|---|
| sunny day, want exercise | 🚶 Walking | Enjoyable, good workout |
| running late for work | 🚲 Biking | 3× faster |
| bad weather | 🚲 Biking | More protected from wind |
