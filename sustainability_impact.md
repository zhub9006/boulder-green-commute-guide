# Sustainability Impact & Environmental Benefits

## Carbon Savings
- Per trip: Switching from drive to bike/walk saves ~0.8 kg CO₂
- Per year (5/wk): ~208 kg CO₂ saved = planting 9.5 trees
- If 50% of Boulder <5km commuters switched: 12,000+ metric tons CO₂/year

## Financial Impact
- Walking: $0/yr. Biking: ~$200/yr. Driving: ~$3,250/yr. Savings: ~$3,050/yr!
- 10 years: ~$15,000-$22,000 saved + ~2,080 kg CO₂

## Health
- Walking: ~400 kcal/trip. 30% reduced heart disease (AHA). 20% longevity boost.
- Biking: ~200 kcal/trip. 40% cardiovascular risk reduction.

## Boulder Incentives
- CO E-Bike Rebate: $1,200 (energystone.colorado.gov/e-bike)
- U-Go Transit Pass: Subsidized RTD
- Xeriscape Rebate: Cash for desert landscaping
