# Tips & Resources for Boulder Green Commuters

## General Tips

### 🌦️ Weather Preparation
- Boulder experiences all four seasons — **dress in layers** for any commute.
- Summer afternoons can bring **thunderstorms** — carry a light rain jacket.
- **Winter mornings** can be icy, especially on 19th Street and north-facing roads.

### 🚲 Biking best practices
- ** Wear a helmet** — Colorado law requires helmets for riders under 18.
- **Use bike lanes** whenever available; they're well-maintained throughout Boulder.
- **Signal your turns** — use hand signals to communicate with drivers.
- **Lock your bike** — use a U-lock and secure it to a fixed object.
- **Bike lights** — use front and rear lights for visibility, especially in early morning or dusk.

### 🦶 Walking tips
- **Wear supportive shoes** — the route is flat but good footwear prevents fatigue.
- **Use crosswalks** — 19th Street has frequent pedestrian crossings.
- **Stay visible** — wear bright or reflective clothing in low-light conditions.

## Boulder-Specific Resources

### 🚲 Bike Infrastructure
- **Boulder Bike Paths:** The city has over 40 miles of paved bike paths.
- **BCycle:** Bike-sharing program with stations throughout Boulder — [bcycle.com](https://www.bcycle.com)
- **Boulder Transportation):** [bouldercase.org](https://www.bouldercase.org) — information on sustainable transportation.

### 🚌 Alternative Modes
- **Boulder Bus:** [RTD Bus](https://www.rtd-denver.com/) serves Boulder with frequent routes.
- **Carpool:** [Boulder Carpool](https://www.bouldercase.org/) — find carpool partners.
- **Skateboard/Scooter:** Boulder is very friendly to e-scooters and skateboards.

### 🗺️ Navigation Apps
- **Google Maps** — has walking and biking directions
- **Strava** — great for tracking bike commutes and joining challenges
- **Komoot** — excellent for discovering new cycling routes
- **Trailforks** — trail-specific navigation for mountain biking

## Community & Advocacy

- **Bicycle Friendly Boulder:** [boulder-cycling.org](https://www.boulder-cycling.org)
- **Walk Boulder:** Advocates for pedestrian-friendly infrastructure
- **Environmental Center:** [environmentalcenter.org](https://www.environmentalcenter.org) — resources for sustainable living
