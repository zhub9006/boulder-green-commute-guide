## 🌍 Why Green Commuting Matters for Boulder

Boulder is known for its commitment to sustainability and outdoor living. Choosing to walk or bike for your commute:

- **Reduces carbon emissions** — Transportation is one of the largest sources of greenhouse gases
- **Improves health** — Regular walking or cycling boosts cardiovascular fitness, mental health, and longevity
- **Saves money** — No fuel costs, no parking fees, reduced vehicle maintenance
- **Reduces traffic congestion** — Fewer cars on the road benefit everyone
- **Supports Boulder's culture** — The city has extensive bike paths, walkable neighborhoods, and a progressive transportation policy
