## 🚶 Walking Route

**Distance:** 4.03 km | **Time:** ~43–48 min

### Turn-by-Turn Directions

| Step | Street | Distance | Time |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| 1 | **Oak Avenue** — Head south from home | 97 m | ~2 min |
| 2 | **19th Street** — Continue south (main corridor) | 2.2 km | ~26 min |
| 3 | **20th St.** — Brief jog east | 106 m | ~1 min |
| 4 | **20th Street** — Continue south | 566 m | ~7 min |
| 5 | **Pearl Street** — Cross east | 216 m | ~3 min |
| 6 | **18th Street** — Continue south | 249 m | ~4 min |
| 7 | **Canyon Boulevard** — Final stretch to office | 637 m | ~8 min |

### Highlights
- Downtown core along 19th Street — vibrant, well-lit pedestrian corridor
- Crosses iconic **Pearl Street Mall** (great for coffee stops!)
- Ends along **Canyon Boulevard** — scenic views of the Flatirons
- Fully sidewalk-equipped with traffic calming features

### Tips
- Carry water — Boulder's elevation (~5,400 ft) accelerates dehydration
- Use sunscreen for exposed sections
- Watch for icy patches in winter

## 🚲 Biking Route

**Distance:** 4.03 km | **Time:** ~12–16 min

### Turn-by-Turn Directions

| Step | Street | Distance | Time |
|------|--------|----------|------|
| 1 | **Oak Avenue** — Depart from home | 97 m | ~30 sec |
| 2 | **19th Street** — Main southbound corridor | 2.2 km | ~3–5 min |
| 3 | **20th St.** — Quick east jog | 106 m | ~15 sec |
| 4 | **20th Street** — Continue south | 566 m | ~1–2 min |
| 5 | **Pearl Street** — Cross east | 216 m | ~30 sec |
| 6 | **18th Street** — Continue south | 249 m | ~30 sec |
| 7 | **Canyon Boulevard** — Arrive at office | 637 m | ~1–2 min |

### Highlights
- **19th Street** has designated bike lanes
- **Canyon Boulevard** features a protected bike path with Flatirons views 🏔️
- Mostly flat terrain with gentle grades
- Bike parking racks along Broadway near 1800

### Tips
- Use bike lights (required by law at night)
- Signal turns — it's the law and courteous
- Canyon Boulevard path is separated from traffic — enjoy the ride!
- Studded tires for winter (snow/ice possible Dec–Feb)
- UV protection essential — altitude = stronger sun
