# 🌿 Boulder Green Commute Guide

Comprehensive guide to sustainable commuting in Boulder, CO — walking and biking routes, travel times, and environmental impact.

## 📍 Route: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway, Boulder, CO
- **Home:** 40.04488°N, 105.27389°W | **Office:** 40.01633°N, 105.27897°W
- **Distance:** 4.03 km (2.5 mi) | **Elevation drop:** ~300 ft (Flatirons slope)

## 🚶 Walking Route (~43–48 min)
1. **Oak Avenue** south — 97 m (~2 min)
2. **19th Street** south — 2.2 km (~26 min) *[wide sidewalk, tree-lined]*
3. **20th St.** east jog — 106 m (~1 min)
4. **20th Street** south — 566 m (~7 min) *[residential stretch]*
5. **Pearl Street** east — 216 m (~3 min) *[cross at crosswalk]*
6. **18th Street** south — 249 m (~4 min) *[pedestrian-friendly corridor]*
7. **Canyon Boulevard** to office — 637 m (~8 min) *[sidewalks, Flatirons views]*

**Highlights:** Pearl Street Mall, Canyon Blvd Flatirons views, fully sidewalk-equipped.
**Tip:** Carry water — Boulder's 5,400 ft elevation accelerates dehydration! Apply sunscreen even on cloudy days.

## 🚲 Biking Route (~12–16 min)
1. **Oak Avenue** south — 97 m (~30 sec)
2. **19th Street** — 2.2 km (~3–5 min) *[bike lanes]*
3. **20th St.** east jog — 106 m (~15 sec)
4. **20th Street** south — 566 m (~1–2 min) *[bike-friendly]*
5. **Pearl Street** east — 216 m (~30 sec) *[cross carefully at Mall]*
6. **18th Street** south — 249 m (~30 sec)
7. **Canyon Boulevard** to office — 637 m (~1–2 min) *[protected path]*

**Tip:** Use bike lights at day and night. Studded tires for winter rides. UV protection is essential at altitude!

## 💡 Tips for New Green Commuters
1. **Start small:** Try 1–2 days/week before committing fully
2. **Layer up:** Boulder's weather changes quickly at 5,400 ft elevation
3. **Morning sun:** East-facing routes get early sun — great for winter commutes
4. **Social commute:** Walk/bike with a coworker for safety and motivation
5. **Track your impact:** Use apps to log CO₂ saved

## 📈 Boulder's Climate Goal
Boulder aims for 80% emission reduction by 2050 (vs. 2005 baseline). Every car-free commute moves us closer!
