# Boulder Green Commute Guide 🌿

*A comprehensive guide to sustainable commuting in Boulder, Colorado — walking and biking route analysis, travel times, and tips for residents looking to reduce their carbon footprint.*

## Sample Commute: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

| Property | Value |
|----------|-------|
| **Origin** | 45 Oak Avenue, Boulder, CO 80304 |
| **Destination** | 1800 Broadway, Boulder, CO 80302 |
| **Distance** | 4.03 km (~2.5 miles) |

## Walking Route 🚶
- **Time:** ~48–52 minutes
- **Distance:** 4.03 km
- **Calories:** ~300–400 kcal
- **CO₂ Saved:** ~0.9 kg per trip

**Route Segments:**
1. Oak Avenue (97 m) — start on a quiet residential street
2. 19th Street (2.2 km) — major southbound connector
3. 20th Street (672 m) — through downtown Boulder
4. Pearl Street (216 m) — iconic shopping district (dismount in mall zone)
5. 18th Street (249 m) — toward the civic area
6. Canyon Boulevard (637 m) — scenic finish with dedicated bike path

## Biking Route 🚲
- **Time:** ~12–15 minutes
- **Distance:** 4.03 km
- **Calories:** ~100–150 kcal
- **CO₂ Saved:** ~0.9 kg per trip

**Same route as walking**, with dedicated bike infrastructure on Canyon Boulevard and through downtown.

## Environmental Impact

Switching from driving to walking/biking saves:
- **~384 kg CO₂ per year** (20 round trips/month) — equivalent to ~19 mature trees
- **$400–500 per year** in fuel costs
- **~1,600 calories/day** (walking) or **~500 calories/day** (biking)

## Boulder Green Commuting Tips

### 🚲 Biking
- Use **Canyon Boulevard** — best dedicated bike infrastructure in Boulder
- **Dismount** on Pearl Street Mall (pedestrian-priority zone)
- Check the [Boulder bike map](https://bouldercolorado.gov/bike) for lane updates
- Always lock your bike — downtown theft is a concern

### 🚶 Walking
- The **Boulder Creek Path** offers a beautiful, low-traffic alternative
- Downtown Boulder is highly walkable with shops and cafes along the way
- Wear reflective gear in winter — short days reduce visibility
- Use pedestrian signals at major intersections

### 🚌 Multi-Modal
- Combine biking with **Boulder Transit** for longer distances
- Consider carpooling or vanpooling for occasional needs
- Even one remote work day per week cuts weekly emissions by 20%
