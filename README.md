## 🚶 Walking Route (45–50 min)
1. **Oak Avenue** south — 97 m (~2 min)
2. **19th Street** south — 2.2 km (~26 min)
3. **20th St.** east jog — 106 m (~1 min)
4. **20th Street** south — 566 m (~7 min)
5. **Pearl Street** east — 216 m (~3 min)
6. **18th Street** south — 249 m (~4 min)
7. **Canyon Boulevard** to office — 637 m (~8 min)

*Highlights: Pearl Street Mall, Canyon Blvd Flatirons views, fully sidewalk-equipped.*
*⚠️ Tip: Carry water — Boulder's 5,400 ft elevation accelerates dehydration!*

## 🚲 Biking Route (12–16 min)
