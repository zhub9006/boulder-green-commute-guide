## 💡 Tips for Sustainable Commuting in Boulder

### Walking Tips
- **Wear reflective gear** — early morning and evening commutes can be dark, especially in winter
- **Carry a rain shell** — Boulder weather can shift quickly
- **Use the paved path along Boulder Creek** when detouring for construction or unsafe crossings
- **Stay hydrated** — the high altitude (5,430 ft) means you lose moisture faster

### Biking Tips
- **Use bike lanes on 19th Street** — the route is most bike-friendly here
- **Lock up securely downtown** — use the bike racks near Pearl Street and the Broadway area
- **Check tire pressure** before your commute — Boulder's altitude can affect rolling resistance
- **Consider a front and rear light** — Colorado law requires a front light for night riding

### General Tips
- **Combine trips** — walk/bike to errands on the way to/from work
- **Buddy up** — find a walking/cycling partner for motivation and safety
- **Track your progress** — use apps like Strava or Google Fit to log your green commutes
- **Weather prep** — Boulder sees snow through March; plan for winter commuting gear
