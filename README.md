# Boulder Green Commute Guide 🌱

A comprehensive, data-driven guide to sustainable commuting options in Boulder, Colorado — helping residents reduce their carbon footprint through walking, biking, and smart travel choices.

## About This Guide

This repository was created to share a real-world commute analysis between **45 Oak Avenue** (Boulder, CO 80304) and **1800 Broadway** (Boulder, CO 80302). Every route, distance, and time estimate is based on OpenStreetMap data and OpenRouteService routing.

## Commute Summary

| Mode | Distance | Estimated Time | CO₂ Saved (vs. driving) |
|------|----------|---------------|------------------------|
| 🚶 Walking | 4.03 km (~2.5 mi) | ~48 min | ~0.7 kg/trip |
| 🚲 Biking | 4.03 km (~2.5 mi) | ~16 min | ~0.7 kg/trip |

> **Note:** Distances are verified via OpenStreetMap routing. Walking times assume a typical pace of 5 km/h; biking times assume an average speed of 15 km/h.

## Why Sustainable Commuting Matters

Boulder is committed to reducing greenhouse gas emissions. Every car trip replaced by walking or biking:
- Reduces CO₂ by approximately **0.7 kg per trip**
- Saves money on fuel and parking
- Improves personal health and fitness
- Reduces traffic congestion and improves air quality
