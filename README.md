## Key Benefits of Green Commuting

### 🌍 Environmental Impact
- Every car trip replaced by walking or biking reduces CO₂ by approximately **0.7 kg per trip**
- Annual CO₂ savings (assuming 250 work days): **~175 kg (385 lbs)**

### 💰 Financial Savings
- Average cost of driving: ~$0.60/mile (fuel, maintenance, parking)
- Walking: $0 | Biking: ~$0.05/mile
- Annual savings: **~$750/year**

### 💪 Health Benefits
- Walking 4 km daily meets the CDC's recommended 150 min/week of moderate exercise
- Biking builds cardiovascular fitness with low joint impact
- Reduced stress from avoiding traffic congestion

## Why Boulder?

Boulder is one of the most bike-friendly cities in the United States:
- 🚲 Over 200 miles of dedicated bike paths and lanes
- 🌄 The Boulder Creek Path provides a scenic, separated bike/pedestrian corridor
- 🏙️ Downtown Boulder is compact and highly walkable
- 🚌 RTD bus routes serve the corridor with bike racks
- 🅿️ Multiple bike parking options along Broadway and downtown
