## 🌤️ Seasonal Recommendations

### Spring–Fall (March–November) ✅
- Ideal for both walking and biking
- Daylight hours are generous
- Boulder's mild climate makes outdoor commuting pleasant
- Bring layers for temperature changes
- Watch for afternoon thunderstorms (June–August)

### Winter (December–February) ❄️
- Walking is still feasible with proper gear (boots, layers)
- Biking requires winter-specific preparations:
  - Studded tires or fat bike for snow/ice
  - Fenders for wet roads
  - Front and rear lights (short daylight hours)
  - Hand warmers and waterproof outerwear
- Most dedicated bike lanes are plowed promptly but side streets may be icy
- Consider combining biking with public transit on the worst weather days
