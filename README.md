## Why Commute Green?

### 🌿 Environmental Impact
- A single 4 km car commute produces approximately **0.8 kg of CO₂** per trip
- Walking or biking produces **zero emissions**
- If all Boulder residents replaced just 10% of their car trips with walking or biking, it would eliminate thousands of tons of CO₂ annually

### 💰 Financial Savings
- Average car operating cost: ~$0.60/km (fuel, maintenance, insurance)
- Annual savings from biking/walking: **~$1,200–$1,800** (based on 200 round-trip commutes/year)

### 🏃 Health Benefits
- Walking 4 km/day meets the recommended 30 minutes of daily physical activity
- Biking improves cardiovascular health and boosts mental well-being
- Boulder residents are among the fittest in the nation!

## Getting Started Tips

### For Walkers:
1. **Wear comfortable shoes** — Boulder's paths are paved and well-maintained
2. **Carry water** — stay hydrated on your walk, especially in Colorado's dry climate
3. **Use a sun hat and sunscreen** — UV exposure at 5,400 ft elevation is strong
4. **Download a podcast or audiobook** — make the 48-minute journey commute time
5. **Buddy up** — walking with a friend makes the time fly

### For Bikers:
1. **Use the Creek Path (Canyon Blvd)** — it's Boulder's premier multi-use path
2. **Follow traffic laws** — treat your bike as a vehicle
3. **Lock your bike** — downtown Boulder has bike racks at most destinations
4. **Check tire pressure** — Colorado's altitude changes can affect tires
5. **Consider a commuter bike** — a simple single-speed or hybrid works great for flat-to-moderate commutes
