## 💡 Tips for Sustainable Commuting in Boulder

1. **Start slow** — Try biking 1–2 days/week and gradually increase
2. **Use B-cycle** — If you don't own a bike, use the city bikeshare
3. **Layer up** — Boulder weather changes quickly; carry a light jacket
4. **Plan for altitude** — At 5,430 ft elevation, exert closer to sea level effort
5. **Check weather** — Afternoon thunderstorms are common in summer
6. **Combine modes** — Bike to RTD, take bus downtown, bike last mile
7. **Join a group** — [Boulder Commuter Club](https://bouldercommuterclub.org/) offers challenges and rewards
