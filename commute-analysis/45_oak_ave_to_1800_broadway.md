## 💡 Route Tips

1. **Best Season:** May–October when Boulder Creek path is clear and dry.
2. **Avoid:** Downtown gridlock on Pearl/18th during Friday evenings and CU Boulder home football games.
3. **Shortcuts:** From 20th Street, you can cut through the Pearl Street Mall alley for a more direct route to Broadway.
4. **Safety First:** Always use hand signals when biking. The 19th Street stretch has regular signalized intersections.
5. **Hydration:** Bring water, especially in summer—Boulder's altitude (5,430 ft) means stronger sun and drier air.
