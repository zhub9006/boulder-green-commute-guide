## 🚲 Biking Route — Turn-by-Turn

**Total Distance:** 4,026.5 meters (~2.5 mi)  
**Estimated Time:** ~16 minutes  
**Difficulty:** Easy (gentle descent, dedicated bike infrastructure where available)  
**Surface:** Mixed — sidewalks, bike lanes, and bike paths

| Step | Street | Distance | Notes |
|------|--------|----------|-------|
| 1 | **Oak Avenue** | 96.6 m (316 ft) | Head south; quiet residential streets = easy start |
| 2 | **19th Street** | 2,156.5 m (7,075 ft) | Main segment — ride south with traffic or use the sidewalk/bike lane; moderate traffic |
| 3 | **20th St. (→ 20th Street)** | 671.7 m (2,204 ft) | Turn left (east); bikes often use this as a bypass for downtown approach |
| 4 | **Pearl Street** | 216.2 m (709 ft) | Pearl is very bike-congested downtown; dismount and walk your bike if needed, OR use the parallel 17th St bike lane instead for a faster, safer option |
| 5 | **18th Street** | 248.8 m (816 ft) | Quick southbound segment; watch for right-turning cars at Broadway |
| 6 | **Canyon Boulevard** | 636.6 m (2,089 ft) | **Bike-friendly!** Canyon Boulevard has a dedicated bike/pedestrian path along Boulder Creek — enjoy the scenery |
| 7 | **Arrival** | — | Arrive at 1800 Broadway (north side of Broadway) |

### 🚲 Biking Safety Notes
- **Best alternative on Pearl:** Instead of riding through Pearl Street mall (very congested), take **17th Street** (parallel, has bike lane) and then cut over to 18th St. This saves time and is much safer.
- **Canyon Boulevard** is the highlight — the dedicated bike path along Boulder Creek makes this segment enjoyable and safe
- **19th Street** has moderate traffic — stay in the bike lane, watch for opening car doors
- **Downtown approach** (18th St → Broadway): Be prepared for pedestrians; Broadway is a multi-lane road — use the crosswalk

### 💡 Pro Tip: Alternative Biking Route
For a **safer and often faster** ride, consider this detour:
> Oak Ave → 19th St → 20th St → **17th Street bike lane** (instead of Pearl) → 18th St → Canyon Blvd → Broadway

This avoids the busy Pearl Street Mall and adds only ~200m while significantly improving safety.
