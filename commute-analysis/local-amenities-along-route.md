# Local Amenities Along the Route: 45 Oak Ave → 1800 Broadway

*Points of interest and amenities along the walking/biking corridor*

---

## Oak Avenue (Starting Point)
- **Residential area** — Quiet, tree-lined street in the Maplewood neighborhood
- **Nearest park:** Mapleton Park (short walk north)
- **Transit:** Route 20 bus stop nearby on Broadway

## 19th Street (Major Corridor)
- **Traffic:** Moderate; well-paved with sidewalks
- **Bike lanes:** Shared lane markings (sharrows) on sections
- **Cross streets:** Intersects with most downtown cross streets
- **Shops:** Small convenience stores and eateries along the corridor

## 20th Street
- **Brief connector** between 19th and Pearl
- **Surface:** Paved, flat
- **Note:** Low-traffic residential connector

## Pearl Street Mall (Downtown)
- **Type:** Pedestrian-only street
- **Length:** ~4 blocks of car-free shopping
- **Amenities:** Coffee shops, restaurants, boutiques, pharmacy, bank
- **Note:** If biking, dismount and walk the bike through the mall, or use parallel streets
- **Closest bike route:** Continue on 20th Street south to 13th, then west to Broadway

## 18th Street
- **Type:** Downtown connector street
- **Direction:** North-south, heading toward 19th/20th
- **Surface:** Paved

## Canyon Boulevard (Destination Area)
- **Boulder Creek Path:** Multi-use paved trail running alongside Boulder Creek
- **Type:** Separated from road traffic — excellent for biking
- **Amenities:** Benches, fountains, public art, creek access
- **Note:** The trail provides a scenic, car-free alternate route during peak traffic hours

## Recommended Stops (Depending on Direction)

### Morning Commute (Home → Office) — Suggestions for a quick stop:
1. **Coffee** — Grab from a café on Pearl Street before heading south
2. **Bike check** — Inspect tires at any downtown bike shop
3. **Weather prep** — Check Boulder Creek Path conditions

### Evening Commute (Office → Home) — Suggestions:
1. **Grocery run** — Natural Grocers on Pearl Street is close to the route
2. **Exercise** — Walk or bike the Boulder Creek Path for extra miles
3. **Dinner** — Pearl Street Mall has diverse restaurants within a block of the route

## Seasonal Notes

| Season | Considerations |
|---|---|
| **Spring (Apr–Jun)** | Pleasant; watch for potholes after winter; wildflowers along Canyon Blvd |
| **Summer (Jul–Sep)** | Hot but dry; carry water; UV protection essential |
| **Fall (Oct–Nov)** | Best biking weather; golden aspens; shorter days |
| **Winter (Dec–Mar)** | Snow/ice possible; use studded tires; bundle up; shorter daylight |
