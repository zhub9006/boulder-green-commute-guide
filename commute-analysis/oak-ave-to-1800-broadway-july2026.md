## 💡 Why This Route Works

1. **19th Street corridor** — Major north-south artery with continuous sidewalks and bike lanes
2. **20th Street connector** — Short, direct link between the north-south streets
3. **Pearl Street district** — Historic pedestrian-friendly zone with cafe culture
4. **Canyon Boulevard / Boulder Creek Path** — Scenic multi-use trail, separated from vehicle traffic, making it ideal for both walking and biking

## 🎯 Boulder Commuting Tips

1. **Use the Boulder Creek Path** — This paved trail runs alongside Boulder Creek and is the most scenic, car-free route option
2. **B-Cycle bike share** — Boulder's public bike-share system; great for trying before you buy
3. **300+ sunny days** — Boulder's climate is ideal for year-round walking and biking; just dress in layers
4. **Bike joke tellers** — Boulder's traffic-calming features make neighborhood streets safer for cyclists
5. **RTD bus + bike** — For longer distances, combine RTD bus with your bike
