- **Front & rear lights** – Especially for dawn/dusk commutes
- **Bell** – Useful on shared pathways like the Creek Path
- **Lock** – U-lock or chain lock for securing your bike at the office
- **Tires:** 700x32c or wider for comfort on shared paths

## 🚦 Traffic & Safety Analysis

### Lowest Risk Segments
1. **Canyon Boulevard / Boulder Creek Path** – Separated multi-use path
2. **Oak Avenue** – Low traffic residential road
3. **20th Street** – Residential, minimal vehicle traffic

### Moderate Risk Segments
1. **19th Street** – Moderate traffic, but has bike lanes
2. **Pearl Street** – Pedestrian-heavy, use caution
3. **18th Street** – Urban area with frequent driveways

### Safety Tips
- Always wear a helmet (save your brain!)
- Use hand signals when turning
- Make eye contact with drivers at intersections
- Be extra cautious on Pearl Street – lots of pedestrians
- In winter, watch for ice on the Creek Path
- Carry a small repair kit (tire lever, patch, mini pump)
