## Quick Tips

### 💰 Savings
- Walking saves **$2,400/year** vs. driving
- Biking saves **$2,350/year** vs. driving
- Even 1 day/week walking saves **~$120/year**

### 🌍 Environment
- Walking or biking removes **~0.7 kg CO₂ per trip**
- 5 days/week biking = **~400 kg CO₂ saved annually**
- That's like **removing your car from the road for 5 months**

### ❤️ Health
- Walking daily meets **CDC's 150 min/week** exercise goal
- Biking builds **cardiovascular fitness** with low joint impact
- No gym membership needed — your commute IS your workout

### 🌤️ Weather
- **Spring/Fall:** Perfect for both modes
- **Summer:** Hot — bring water, early morning better
- **Winter:** Walking works with proper gear; biking needs studded tires/fenders/lights
