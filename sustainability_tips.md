## Practical Green Commuting Tips

### 1. Start with a 50/50 Split
- Try walking or biking just 2-3 days per week to start
- Use BCycle bike share for days when your bike needs maintenance
- Combine modes: bike to the Creek Path, walk the last few blocks if needed

### 2. Bike Maintenance Basics
- Check tire pressure weekly (altitude changes affect pressure)
- Lubricate chain monthly (dry climate = faster chain wear)
- Keep brake pads adjusted (gravel dust is abrasive)
- Store bike indoors if possible (temperature swings cause condensation)

### 3. Walking Comfort
- Proper footwear: Replace walking shoes every 500-800 km (pavement is hard in Boulder)
- Moisture-wicking socks prevent blisters (dry climate = cracked skin)
- Vaseline or anti-chafe balm on feet before long walks
- Reflective gear for dawn/dusk commutes (low winter light)

### 4. Safety First
- **Bike:** Always wear a helmet (Boulder law requires under 18, but always recommended)
- **Bike:** Use lights front and rear, even during daytime (canyon path can be shaded)
- **Walk:** Stay on the Canyon Boulevard multi-use path; keep right, pass left
- **Bike:** Signal turns, make eye contact with drivers at intersections

### 5. Seasonal Strategy
- **Winter:** Studded bike tires, traction devices for walkers, fenders on bike
- **Summer:** Hydration pack for walking; sun-protective clothing for biking
- **Fall:** Best commuting weather — enjoy the golden aspens!
- **Year-round:** Keep commuting gear visible and accessible (hooks/shoes by door)

- **Rebate Amount:** $1,200
- **Eligibility:** Colorado resident purchasing new e-bike
- **Website:** letsrhobike.com
- **Route Suitability:** Excellent — flat 4 km is ideal for Class 1 e-bike
- **Energy Cost Comparison:** $0 (bike) vs $500+/year (car gas + parking)

- **Stations Near Route:**
  - Pearl & 11th (450 m from route)
  - 18th & Pearl (600 m from route)
  - 13th & Broadway (1.1 km from route)

## Making It Stick: Behavioral Tips

1. **Track your commutes** — Use Strava, Komoot, or JouleBug to gamify your progress
2. **Set a "Green Commute Day"** — Pick one day a week as your green commute day
3. **Track your savings** — Log costs and CO₂ saved to stay motivated
4. **Find a commute buddy** — Walking or biking with someone makes it more fun
5. **Celebrate milestones** — After 1 month, 3 months, 1 year of green commuting
6. **Share your story** — Post on social media with #BoulderGreenCommute to inspire others
