# Boulder Commute Resources

## Sustainable Transportation Options in Boulder

### 🚲 Bike Resources
- **Boulder Creek Path** — A scenic, car-free multi-use path running through the heart of Boulder
- **Boulder B-cycle** — Electric bike-sharing system with stations throughout downtown
- **CU Bike Library** — Free or discounted bike rentals for CU students and staff
- **Boulder Bike Co-op** — Community bike shop for repairs and gear

### 🚶 Walkability
- **Pearl Street Mall** — Car-free pedestrian mall in downtown Boulder
- **Downtown Boulder** — Highly walkable with shops, restaurants, and cafes
- **CU Boulder Campus** — Extensive pedestrian pathways and walkways

### 🚌 Transit
- **RTD Bus** — Regional transit connecting Boulder to Denver and surrounding areas
- **Boulder SkyRide** — Seasonal bus service to ski areas
- **CU Bus** — Free bus service around CU Boulder campus

### 🅿️ Parking
- **Downtown parking garages** — Multiple options along Pearl Street and Canyon Boulevard
- **Street parking** — Metered parking available in downtown areas
- **Bike parking** — Abundant bike racks throughout downtown Boulder
