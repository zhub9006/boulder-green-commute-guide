## The Bottom Line

### 🥇 Recommended: Bike 3-5 days/week
- **16 minutes each way** (3× faster than walking)
- **Free** with your own bike (or $3.50 with B-Cycle)
- **0 kg CO₂** — fully sustainable
- **~200 kcal/day** in moderate cardio
- **Canyon Boulevard** has a separated bike lane — safest riding in Boulder
- Apply for the **$1,200 Colorado E-Bike Rebate** to make it easier!

### 🥈 Good for: Walk 2-3 days/week
- **~48 minutes each way** — great for exercise and mental prep
- **400 kcal burned** — meets daily exercise needs
- **Free** — no equipment needed
- **Most pleasant:** Oak Avenue (quiet, shaded) and Canyon Blvd (scenic, safe)
- **Most challenging:** 19th Street (busy, 53% of the route)

### 🚗 For bad weather: RTD Bus + Walk
- **~12 minutes** with traffic avoidance
- **$3.50/trip** or **$99/month** unlimited
