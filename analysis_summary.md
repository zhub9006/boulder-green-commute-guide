### The One Challenge on 19th Street

**19th Street is the critical segment** — 54% of your route. Here's the reality:

For walking: The sidewalks are well-maintained, but 2,157 m on a busy arterial with RTD bus traffic means you'll share space with buses and need to watch for right-turning vehicles at every intersection.

For biking: The 4-ft painted bike lane on 19th Street is the **dooring zone**. Ride in the lane (not near parked cars), be predictable, and use hand signals. Traffic lights at Euclid and 21st will cause delays.

**If 19th Street feels intimidating:** Try walking/biking the residential path (20th Street corridor) on weekends or off-peak hours, then switch to RTD bus on 19th Street. The Route 1/3/7/10 runs every 15 minutes on 19th Street.
