## 💡 Top Tips for This Commute

1. 🕐 **Timing:** Bike before 8:30 AM or after 4:30 PM to avoid 19th St rush hour
2. 🌧️ **Weather:** Carry a rain jacket — summer afternoons (May–Sep) bring common thunderstorms
3. ❄️ **Winter:** Boulder clears major bike routes aggressively; studded tires recommended Nov–Mar
4. 🚲 **Bike parking:** B-Cycle stations and bike racks available near 1800 Broadway — check office facilities
5. 💧 **Hydration:** Boulder's 5,430 ft elevation dehydrates you faster — carry water!
