## 🗺️ How to Use This Guide

### Morning Commute Options
1. **Coffee first:** Grab a latte at Capital One Cafe or Amante before heading south on 19th St
2. **Quick stop:** Ku Cha House of Tea is perfect for a mid-morning tea break if working late
3. **Post-commute errand:** Natural Grocers on Broadway for grocery stop

### Evening Return Options
1. **Dinner walk:** Jax Fish House, Pasta Jay's, or Sushi Zanmai are steps from the office
2. **Bike repair:** University Bicycles on Pearl St (open until 6PM Mon–Sat)
3. **Social stop:** Ben & Jerry's for ice cream or The Corner Bar for a drink

### Emergency Needs
- **Bike flat/mechanical:** University Bicycles (downtown) or carry a CO2 inflator
- **Phone dead/Internet needed:** Capital One Cafe (free Wi-Fi & outlets)
- **Medical:** Pharmaca (Integrative Pharmacy) on Broadway
