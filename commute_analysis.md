### Elevation Profile
- Start (Oak Ave): ~5,438 ft (1,658 m)
- End (Broadway): ~5,453 ft (1,662 m)
- **Max gain: ~15 m (50 ft)** — essentially flat!
- The route flows gently downhill toward Broadway — no challenging hills.

### Traffic & Safety Notes
- **19th Street** is the busiest segment: 4 lanes, moderate traffic. Use crosswalks and watch for right-turning vehicles at intersections.
- **Canyon Boulevard** has a painted/protected bike lane — this is the safest part of the route.
- **Pearl Street** has heavy foot traffic near the Mall; dismount or slow down in the afternoon.
- **Night riding**: Use front headlight + rear reflector. Canyon Blvd is better lit than 19th St.

### Bus Hybrid Option
- **RTD Bus on 19th Street** (routes 1, 3, 7, 10): Bus from nearby stop at 19th & Pearl → Broadway. ~12 min by bus + 5 min walk = ~17 min total. This could replace biking on rainy days.
- **B-Cycle**: Stations at Broadway & Iris and Broadway & 13th. ~$8 for a day pass or ~$80/month. Rent a bike from a station near home and return at work.
