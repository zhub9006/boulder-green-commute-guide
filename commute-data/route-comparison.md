## Break-Even Analysis

### When does biking save time vs. driving?
- **Bike:** 16 min + 2 min (lock/unlock/park) = ~18 min total
- **Drive:** 10 min + 3 min (search for parking) + 1 min (walk from lot) = ~14 min total
- **Biking is ~4 min slower per trip**, BUT you gain 16 min of exercise per trip!
- **Net time value:** If you value exercise at just ~15¢/min, biking "saves" time compared to driving + gym.

### When does walking save money?
- Walking is always free. Biking costs ~$0.40/month. Driving costs ~$30–40/month.
- **Annual savings vs. driving:** Walking = $360+, Biking = ~$355

### Environmental ROI
- Switch from driving to biking: **10 trees worth of CO₂ saved per year**
- Switch from driving to walking: Same savings, plus more exercise!

## Boulder-Specific Advantages

1. 🚲 **Bike infrastructure is excellent** — Boulder consistently ranks among the top 5 bike-friendly cities in the US
2. ⛰️ **Short distances** — Most Boulder errands are within a 5 km radius
3. 🌲 **Scenic routes** — The Boulder Creek Path is one of America's best urban bike trails
4. 🏙️ **Compact downtown** — Downtown is walkable; no need for a car for daily needs
5. 🌡️ **300+ days of sunshine** — Year-round cycling is viable with proper gear
6. 🏔️ **Altitude** — At 5,430 ft, you get a natural workout bonus (more calories burned at altitude)
