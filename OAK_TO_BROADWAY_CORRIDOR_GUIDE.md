# 🗺️ Oak Avenue → Broadway Corridor Guide

**Complete walking & biking corridor guide for 45 Oak Avenue to 1800 Broadway, Boulder CO**

> **Route:** 45 Oak Avenue → 19th Street → 20th Street → Pearl Street Mall → 18th Street → Canyon Boulevard → Broadway  
> **Total Distance:** 4.03 km (2.5 miles)  
> **Elevation Change:** ~20 m descent (1,580 m → 1,560 m) — a gentle downhill on the return!

## 📋 Turn-by-Turn Walking Directions

### Segment 1: Oak Avenue (Home to 19th Street)
- **Distance:** 97 m (317 ft) — ~1 minute
- **Directions:** Head south on Oak Avenue from your home at 1800 Oak Avenue. The sidewalk is well-maintained with mature shade trees.
- **Terrain:** Flat, residential street with light traffic.
- **Notes:** Quiet start to your commute — enjoy the neighborhood feel.

### Segment 2: 19th Street (Main Corridor)
- **Distance:** 2.16 km (1.34 mi) — ~33 minutes walking
- **Directions:** Turn left (east) onto 19th Street. This is your main corridor for 2.16 km through the heart of Boulder.
- **Terrain:** Moderate decline (heading east/south toward downtown). Sidewalks on both sides.
- **Key Crossings:**
  - 19th & Folsom — right-turning vehicles, watch carefully
  - 19th & 20th — dedicated bike signal and turn lane
  - 19th & Kalmia — school zone (Centennial Middle School nearby)
  - 19th & Redwood — residential intersection
- **RTD Bus Stops Along 19th St:**
  | Stop | Location | Direction | Stop Code |
  |------|----------|-----------|-----------|
  | 19th St & Poplar Ave | 19th & Poplar | Northbound | 10388 |
  | 19th St & Poplar Ave | 19th & Poplar | Southbound | 10387 |
  | 19th St & Norwood Ave | 19th & Norwood | Northbound | 10384 |
  | 19th St & Norwood Ave | 19th & Norwood | Southbound | 10385 |
  | 19th St & Kalmia Ave | 19th & Kalmia | Southbound | 33174 |
  | 19th St & Redwood Ave | 19th & Redwood | Southbound | 10390 |
- **Notes:** Watch for pedestrians, especially near schools and downtown approach.

### Segment 3: 20th Street Connector
- **Distance:** 106 m (348 ft) — ~12 seconds
- **Directions:** A short connector between 19th and 20th Street. Follow the bike symbol / pedestrian signs.
- **Terrain:** Flat, brief segment.

### Segment 4: 20th Street
- **Distance:** 566 m (1,857 ft) — ~1 min 9 sec walking
- **Directions:** Continue south on 20th Street toward downtown.
- **Terrain:** Slight descent into downtown.

### Segment 5: Pearl Street Mall ⚠️ PEDESTRIAN ZONE
- **Distance:** 216 m (709 ft) — ~26 seconds walking (bikers: dismount & walk)
- **Directions:** Enter the Pearl Street Mall at 20th/Pearl intersection. Enjoy the car-free promenade!
- **⚠️ Biker Alert:** Pearl Street Mall is a **pedestrian-only zone** — you must dismount and walk your bike through. This is actually a pleasant break from urban traffic.
- **Nearby Stops & Amenities on Pearl Street:**
  - **Capital One Cafe** (1247 Pearl St) — Coffee, open Mo–Fr 7:00 AM, wheelchair accessible
  - **Ku Cha House of Tea** (1211 Pearl St) — Tea house, open daily
  - **Ben & Jerry's** (1203 Pearl St Mall) — Ice cream, outdoor seating available
  - **Jax Fish House** (928 Pearl St) — Seafood restaurant
  - **Pasta Jay's** (1001 Pearl St) — Italian cuisine
- **Tip:** If you're walking, this is a great section to take your time and explore the downtown vibe.

### Segment 6: 18th Street
- **Distance:** 249 m (817 ft) — ~37 seconds walking
- **Directions:** Exit the Pearl Mall onto 18th Street heading west toward Broadway.
- **Terrain:** Brief flat segment. Bike racks available at 18th & Canyon Blvd.

### Segment 7: Canyon Boulevard to Broadway (Destination)
- **Distance:** 637 m (2,090 ft) — ~1 minute walking
- **Directions:** Turn south on Canyon Boulevard (the dedicated cycling corridor). Cross Broadway to reach 1800 Broadway.
- **Terrain:** Moderate descent with bike-protected lanes.
- **Notes:** Canyon Boulevard has buffered bike lanes — a highlight of this commute. Views of the Flatirons on clear days!

### Biking Gear Recommendations
- **Lights:** Front white + rear red, required by law and essential even in daylight at 5,200 ft
- **Helmet:** MIPS-equipped recommended (Boulder has a strong helmet culture)
- **U-Lock:** Required for bike racks at 1800 Broadway (Canyon Blvd area)
- **Tires:** Standard road/urban tires in summer; studded tires Nov–Feb
- **BCycle Option:** Day pass ~$9/day — bikes available at stations near both walk/bike start and end points
