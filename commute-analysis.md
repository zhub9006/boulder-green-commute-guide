## Walking Analysis 🚶

### Pros
- **Zero emissions** — completely carbon-free
- **Health benefits** — ~250 calories burned per trip
- **No parking needed** — walk directly to your door
- **Mental health** — stress reduction, fresh air, neighborhood awareness
- **Free** — no fuel, no maintenance costs

### Cons
- **Time** — ~50 minutes each way (100 min round trip)
- **Weather exposure** — no protection from rain, snow, or extreme heat
- **Safety** — need to be visibility-aware, especially at dawn/dusk

### Recommendations
- Wear reflective gear in low-light conditions
- Carry a small daypack with rain layer
- Consider a hat/sunscreen in summer
- Use sidewalks and crosswalks — Boulder is very pedestrian-friendly

---

## Biking Analysis 🚲

### Pros
- **Zero emissions** — completely carbon-free
- **Fast** — ~15 minutes, only 50% longer than driving
- **Health benefits** — moderate exercise, ~50 calories burned
- **Parking** — easy bike parking at Broadway downtown
- **Free** — no fuel, no parking fees

### Cons
- **Weather** — rain, wind, and cold can be challenging
- **Safety** — share roads with vehicles; use bike lanes
- **Carrying capacity** — limited vs. a car
- **Bike maintenance** — occasional chain, tire, brake adjustments

### Recommendations
- Invest in a good helmet and lights (front + rear)
- Use dedicated bike lanes wherever available
- Plan for weather: fenders, rain jacket, layers
- Register your bike with Boulder PD for theft recovery
- Consider an e-bike for hill assistance or sweat-free arrival
