The Canyon Boulevard segment follows the **Boulder Creek Path**, a 3.2 km scenic, car-free, paved multi-use trail that runs alongside Boulder Creek. This is one of Boulder's most cherished green infrastructure assets:
- Completely separated from automobile traffic
- Paved and accessible for all abilities
- Offers iconic Flatirons views
- Photography-worthy scenery
- Well-lit and patrolled
- Connects north Boulder to the downtown core
