# 🏪 Route Amenities Guide: Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

Knowledge of nearby amenities makes your green commute more enjoyable and practical.

## Walker-Friendly Amenities Along the Route

|----------|------|---------------------|-------|

### Rest Stops & Water
- **Boulder Creek** — Runs parallel to parts of the route; great for a quick splash in summer
- **Pearl Street Mall** — Public fountains and benches throughout the pedestrian mall
- **City Park** — Off-route but nearby for a longer rest break

### Bike-Friendly Stops

## Seasonal Amenity Tips

### Summer (June–September)
- Carry water — Boulder's altitude accelerates dehydration
- Early morning walks/rides avoid mid-day heat
- Pearl Street outdoor cafes are ideal rest stops

### Fall (September–November)
- Cooler temps make for ideal commuting weather
- Aspen leaf season adds scenic beauty

### Winter (December–February)
- Check sidewalk clearing conditions on 19th Street
- Icy bridges near Boulder Creek require extra caution on bike
- Layer up — morning commutes can be 25°F colder than afternoon

### Spring (March–May)
- Wildflower season along Boulder Creek
- Rain gear essential — afternoon showers are common
- Bike lanes clear quickly after rain

## Accessibility Notes
- **Sidewalk coverage:** Good on all segments of the route
- **Crosswalk signals:** Present at all major intersections (19th, 20th, Pearl, 18th, Canyon)
- **Curb ramps:** Available at all intersections
- **Accessibility for mobility devices:** Route is largely flat with gentle grades
