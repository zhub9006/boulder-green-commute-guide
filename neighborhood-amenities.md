- **RTD Bus Routes 1, 3, 7, 10** — Frequent service along 19th Street

### Along Pearl Street (downtown)
- **Pearl Street Mall** — Historic pedestrian-only zone with over 200 shops and restaurants
- **Multiple B-Cycle stations** — Bike-share at 13th & Pearl, 15th & Pearl
- **Café Row** — Capital One Cafe, Ku Cha Tea, Tea and Cakes, Sweetgreen
- **Restaurant Row** — Jax Fish House, Pasta Jay's, Ben & Jerry's, Pearl Street restaurants

### Along Canyon Boulevard (Creek Path)
- **Boulder Creek Path** — 3.2 km scenic, car-free, paved multi-use trail
- **Flatirons views** — Iconic Boulder views along this segment
- **Boulder County Sheriff HQ** — Near 1800 Broadway, adds safety to the downtown area
