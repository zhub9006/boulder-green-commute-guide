## 🚦 Boulder-Specific Commuting Tips

1. **Boulder Creek Path** — The paved multi-use trail is separated from road traffic and offers a scenic, car-free alternative along Boulder Creek.
2. **Bike joke tellers** — Boulder's traffic calming infrastructure makes biking safer and more enjoyable.
3. **Downtown parking** — If you drive occasionally, use the City Center parking structure (~$1/hr) or the 13th Street garage.
4. **B-Cycle bike share** — Boulder's public bike-share means you can try cycling without owning a bike.
5. **Weather** — Boulder is sunny 300+ days/year. Carry a light rain layer and dress in layers.
6. **Walk-bike combo** — On bad weather days, walk the flatter sections and bike the downhill portions.
7. **RTD Bus** — For longer distances, RTD buses have bike racks so you can combine modes.
