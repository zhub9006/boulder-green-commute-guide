## 📅 Week 1: Reconnaissance & Gear Up

**Goal:** Familiarize yourself with the route without the pressure of a full commute.

| Day | Activity | Time/Wk | Notes |
|-----|----------|---------|-------|
| Mon | **Scout the route on foot** — walk the full 4 km at leisure | ~50 min | Note intersections,自行车的路况喜欢的休息点 |
| Tue | **Scout the route on bike** — ride the full distance | ~15 min | Test your bike, check tire pressure, brakes |
| Wed | **Identify gear needs** — buy waterproof jacket, helmet, lights | — | Priority items: reflective vest, U-lock, water bottle |
| Thu | **Pack a trial commute bag** — clothes, shoes, rain gear, lunch | — | Keep it by the door so you're ready |
| Fri | **Walk or bike for one errand** near your office | — | Build the habit on a low-stakes day |
| Sat | **Explore alternative routes** — try a different path for variety | — | Boulder Creek Path, 13th Street corridor |
| Sun | **Rest & plan** — review the week, adjust gear, check weather forecast | — | Prep clothes for Monday |

### 🛒 Gear Checklist
- [ ] Waterproof jacket (Boulder averages 30" precipitation/year)
- [ ] Cycling helmet (CO law requires it)
- [ ] Front & rear bike lights
- [ ] U-lock + secondary cable lock
- [ ] Water bottle (5400 ft elevation = faster dehydration)
- [ ] Sunscreen (high-altitude UV is ~25% stronger per 1000 ft)
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes (if walking)
- [ ] Change of clothes at office (optional)

## 📅 Week 2: Start Small — 1–2 Days/Week

**Goal:** Replace 1–2 driving commutes with walking or biking.

| Day | Mode | Duration | Tips |
|-----|------|----------|------|
| Mon | **Bike** | ~16 min | Start with the easiest segment — Oak Ave to 19th St is quiet residential |
| Tue | **Drive** | — | Use the "test day" to compare how you feel |
| Wed | **Walk** | ~50 min | Treat it as a morning walk — listen to a podcast or music |
| Thu | **Drive** | — | Note parking cost and time spent searching for spots |
| Fri | **Bike** | ~16 min | End the week with a fast, energizing ride |
| Sat/Sun | **Off** | — | Enjoy a car-free weekend if possible |

### 📝 Week 2 Reflection Questions
1. How did I feel arriving at work — more energized or more tired?
2. Did I encounter any safety issues? Where?
3. Was the weather manageable? What would I add to my gear?
4. How much time did I save/lose compared to driving?
5. Would I do this again tomorrow?

### 🔄 Optimizing Your Routine
- **Shower at work?** Confirm your office has locker/shower facilities
- **Lunch on the go?** Identify cafes near the route on Pearl Street
- **Weather backup?** Check RTD bus schedule for rain/snow days
- **Bike parking?** Confirm 1800 Broadway has secure bike racks

## 🌦️ Seasonal Adjustments

| Season | Walking Tips | Biking Tips |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **☀️ Summer (Jun–Aug)** | Hydrate more; UV is intense at 5,400 ft | Early morning rides avoid heat; carry 2L water |
| **🍂 Fall (Sep–Nov)** | Perfect walking weather; layer up | Great biking conditions; shorter days need lights |
| **❄️ Winter (Dec–Feb)** | Wear insulated layers; watch for ice on sidewalks | Studded tires, thermal gloves, full-finger hood; consider RTD backup |
| **🌸 Spring (Mar–May)** | Rain jacket essential; trails may be muddy | Mud on paths; check Boulder Creek Path conditions |
