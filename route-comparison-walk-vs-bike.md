## 📈 The "Best of Both Worlds" Option

Consider a **bike/walk split**:

- **Walk** in the morning (arrive energized) → **Bike** home (arrive fast, watch the sunset on Canyon Blvd)
- Or vice versa: **Bike** in the morning (get there fast) → **Walk** home (decompress after work)

This gives you variety, flexibility, and lets you choose the best mode for each direction based on your schedule and the weather forecast.
