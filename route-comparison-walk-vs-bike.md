---

## When to Walk 🚶
- ✅ Fantastic weather
- ✅ Short on time but still want exercise
- ✅ Need to arrive at work fresh (no sweat)
- ✅ Carrying items that are awkward on a bike
- ✅ You're coming from/going to the north end (near CU Boulder)

## When to Bike 🚴
- ✅ Running late — 14 min beats 50 min drastically
- ✅ Combination with B-Cycle (rent e-bikes)
- ✅ Canyon Boulevard path is the best part of either route
- ✅ Year-round with proper gear
- ✅ Want to explore Boulder's bike infrastructure

---

## 🔀 Smart Combination Strategy

Consider a **hybrid commute**:
- **Bike to work, walk back home** on alternating days
- **Walk on rainy days, bike on clear days**
- **Use B-Cycle e-bikes** (available throughout downtown Boulder) for the 19th Street to Broadway segment while walking through the more residential north section
