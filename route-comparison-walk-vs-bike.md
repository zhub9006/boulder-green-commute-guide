## When to Choose Walking

✅ You want a workout before work  
✅ You're carrying items that won't fit on a bike  
✅ The weather is bad (rain, snow, extreme heat)  
✅ You have extra time in the morning  
✅ You want a meditative, phone-free start to your day  
✅ Your legs need the exercise more than your lungs  

## When to Choose Biking

✅ You're short on time in the morning  
✅ You want to arrive energized, not exhausted  
✅ The weather is mild (spring/fall)  
✅ You have a longer commute on other days and want variety  
✅ You're carrying a laptop/bag that fits in a pannier  
✅ You want to take advantage of Boulder's bike infrastructure  

---

## The Hybrid Approach

Many Boulder residents combine both modes throughout the week:

- **Mon/Wed/Fri:** Bike (fast, efficient)
- **Tue/Thu:** Walk (exercise, mindfulness)
- **Friday evening bike home:** Social rides with coworkers

This gives you the benefits of both while keeping your commute interesting year-round.

---

## Combining with Transit

RTD Bus routes also serve this corridor. On days with extreme weather:

1. **Walk to the nearest bus stop** (RTD Route 201/202 on Arapahoe or Broadway)
2. **Ride to downtown Boulder**
3. **Walk the last few blocks to 1800 Broadway**

This is a great winter backup option.
