## When to Walk

- ✅ You want a calming, meditative start/end to your day
- ✅ You need to carry items (laptop bag, lunch, groceries)
- ✅ The weather is poor (rain, snow) and you want to stay dry
- ✅ You're recovering from injury or have lower fitness
- ✅ You want maximum calorie burn
- ✅ You're heading to downtown for a meeting (less noticeable arrival)

## When to Bike

- ✅ You're pressed for time and need to arrive quickly
- ✅ You're already in good cardiovascular shape
- ✅ You have a proper bike and gear (helmet, lights, rain gear)
- ✅ You want to save the most money on commuting costs
- ✅ You're carrying a lightweight load (backpack only)
- ✅ The weather is clear and temperatures are comfortable
- ✅ You want to explore Boulder's bike infrastructure

## The Hybrid Option: Bike + Walk Combo
