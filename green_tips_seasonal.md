# 🌦️ Seasonal Green Commuting Tips for Boulder

**Route:** 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway (4.03 km)
**Elevation:** ~5,430 ft (1,655 m) — high altitude!

---

## Spring (April – June) 🌸
- Mild temperatures: 50–75°F (10–24°C)
- Low precipitation, occasional afternoon thunderstorms
- ✅ Ideal season — perfect walking and biking weather
- Bring a light rain jacket for afternoon storms
- Enjoy the American Elm canopy on Oak Avenue

## Summer (July – September) ☀️
- Dry heat: 75–95°F (24–35°C)
- ⚠️ Start early (before 8 AM) to beat the heat
- Carry water — at 5,430 ft, dehydration happens fast
- Wear sunscreen and a hat (UV index 8-10+)
- Canyon Boulevard has shade from cottonwood trees

## Fall (October – November) 🍂
- Crisp, cool air: 40–65°F (4–18°C)
- ✅ Ideal season — cool temps, beautiful golden aspens
- Layer up — mornings can be chilly, afternoons mild
- Shorter days — consider a reflective vest or light after 5 PM

## Winter (December – March) ❄️
- Cold: 15–45°F (-9–7°C)
- Snow and ice possible, bike lanes may be covered
- ⚠️ Challenges: ice on sidewalks, cold gear needed
- Alternative: RTD Bus on 19th Street + walk last blocks (~12 min)

---

## High-Altitude Considerations ⛰️

| Effect | Impact | Mitigation |
|--------|--------|------------|
| Faster dehydration | Lose water 2× faster than sea level | Drink water before, during, after commute |
| Stronger UV | Sunburn in 15 min at altitude | SPF 30+, sunglasses, hat |
| Thinner air | More wind exposure, cooler feels | Windproof layers, face covering in winter |
| Fatigue | 1-2 week adjustment period | Gradually increase active commuting |

---

## Gear Recommendations

| Season | Walking Gear | Biking Gear |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| Spring | Light layers, rain shell, comfortable shoes | Light jacket, gloves, pannier bag |
| Summer | Sun hat, sunscreen, water bottle | Sun sleeves, sunglasses, water bottle cage |
| Fall | Fleece layer, reflective vest, comfortable shoes | Layered clothing, lights (shorter days) |
| Winter | Insulated boots, thermal layers, hat, gloves | Studded tires, fenders, thermal layers, lights |
