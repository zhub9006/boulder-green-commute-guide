**Weather:** Colorado weather changes quickly — carry layers and rain gear year-round. Summer afternoons bring thunderstorms.

**Bike Parking:** 1800 Broadway area has bike racks — always use a quality U-lock or chain lock.

**Seasonal Tips:**
- ☀️ **Summer (Jun–Aug):** Best riding/walking weather; watch for afternoon thunderstorms after 2 PM
- 🍂 **Fall (Sep–Nov):** Ideal cycling and walking weather with comfortable temperatures
- ❄️ **Winter (Dec–Feb):** Watch for ice on sidewalks; use studded tires on bikes; shorter days require lights
