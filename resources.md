# 🚌 Boulder Transit & Transportation Resources

## RTD Bus

- **Route 200 (Pearl Street Mall)** — Runs along Pearl Street, connecting the south end (downtown Broadway) to the north end (CU Boulder campus area). Trip time: ~15 min.
- **Route 1 (Broadway)** — Connects downtown to Pine Street and the northside via Broadway
- **RTD Trip Planner:** [https://www.rtd-denver.com](https://www.rtd-denver.com)
- **RTD App:** Download for real-time bus tracking

## B-Cycle

- **Seattle Street Station** (near Pearl St) — Capital Bikeshare equivalent for Boulder
- **U.H. Station** — Near CU Boulder
- **B-Cycle App:** [https://www.b-cycle.com](https://www.b-cycle.com)
- **Passes:** Day passes, monthly, and annual passes available
- **Use for:** Bad weather days, when you're running late, or return trip leg

## 🚗 Car Alternatives

| Option | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Park & Ride** | Pearl Street Garage → bus to CU Hill | 300+ spaces | ~$3/day |
| **Carpool** | 19th St corridor has HOV lanes during rush hour |
| **EV Charging** | Several stations in downtown Boulder (Pearl St area) |

## 🚲 Bike Infrastructure

- **200+ miles of paved bike paths** in Boulder
- **Canyon Boulevard** — Dedicated bike lane (used in this commute)
- **19th Street** — Marked bike lane
- **Pearl Street Mall** — Shared lane, pedestrian priority
- **Bike parking:** Multiple racks near 1800 Broadway (within 100 m)

## 📱 Useful Apps

- **Google Maps** — Turn-by-turn walking/biking directions
- **AllTrails** — For scenic alternate routes via Boulder Creek Path
- **B-Cycle app** — Bike share availability
- **RTD app** — Real-time bus tracking
- **Strava** — Track your commute and compare with others
