# 🚌 Boulder Transit & Sustainable Travel Resources

## RTD (Regional Transportation District)
- **Website**: [rtd-denver.com](https://www.rtd-denver.com/facilities/boulder)
- **Phone**: 303-275-7433
- **Routes serving 80304/80302**: Several routes connect North Boulder to downtown
- **Bus with bike rack**: All RTD buses have front-mounted bike racks (free!)

## B-Cycle (Bike Share)
- **Stations near 45 Oak Ave**: Check B-Cycle Boulder station map
- **Stations near 1800 Broadway**: Multiple downtown stations
- **App**: Download the B-Cycle app for real-time station availability
- **Day pass**: $8, Hot Spot membership: $80/year

## City of Boulder Transportation
- **Bike maps**: [bouldercolorado.gov/bike](https://www.bouldercolorado.gov/bike)
- **Safe Routes to School**: Maps and crossing guards
- **Traffic calming reports**: Street-level safety data

## Employer Resources
- Check if your employer offers **transit benefit cards** or **bike-to-work subsidies**
- Many Boulder employers provide **shower/changing facilities** for bike commuters
- **Remote work options**: Even 1–2 days/week from home reduces commute emissions significantly
