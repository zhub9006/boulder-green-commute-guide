## Key Takeaways

- **Walk when:** Weather is nice, you have time, or you want exercise and mental clarity
- **Bike when:** You're short on time, weather is reasonable, or you want a quick, invigorating trip
- **Either way:** You're contributing to a healthier Boulder and a greener planet 🌍
