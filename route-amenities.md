## 🌳 Route Comfort Tips

1. **Start your walk with coffee:** Grab a latte at Pekoe Sip House (opens 6 AM) before heading down 19th Street.
2. **Mid-route snack:** Breadworks on Broadway is a great stop if you're hungry halfway through.
3. **Bike-share backup:** If your bike gets a flat or you're caught in rain, the B-cycle station on Broadway offers quick rental options.
4. **Pharmacy stop:** Pharmaca on Broadway is open late and carries bike repair supplies and first-aid essentials.
5. **Restrooms:** Public toilets are available along the corridor for longer walking commutes.
