# Biking Route Details

**Route**: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway, Boulder
**Total Distance**: 4.03 km (2.51 miles)
**Waypoints**: Oak Avenue (start) → Canyon Boulevard (end)

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### Leg 1: Oak Avenue
- **Distance**: 97 meters
- **Duration**: ~16 seconds
- **Description**: Begin at 45 Oak Avenue heading south.

### Leg 2: 19th Street
- **Distance**: 2.16 km (1.34 miles)
- **Duration**: ~3.5 minutes
- **Description**: Turn onto 19th Street and head south. This is the longest segment — stay alert for pedestrians and cross traffic.

### Leg 3: 20th Street (Short Connector)
- **Distance**: 106 meters
- **Duration**: ~12 seconds
- **Description**: Brief connector onto 20th Street.

### Leg 4: 20th Street (Continued)
- **Distance**: 566 meters
- **Duration**: ~1 minute
- **Description**: Continue along 20th Street heading south.

### Leg 5: Pearl Street
- **Distance**: 216 meters
- **Duration**: ~26 seconds
- **Description**: Turn onto Pearl Street, Boulder's popular downtown area.

### Leg 6: 18th Street
- **Distance**: 249 meters
- **Duration**: ~37 seconds
- **Description**: Turn onto 18th Street heading south.

### Leg 7: Canyon Boulevard (Final Approach)
- **Distance**: 637 meters
- **Duration**: ~59 seconds
- **Description**: Turn onto Canyon Boulevard and arrive at 1800 Broadway.

## Biking Tips

- ✅ **Best for**: Fast, efficient daily commute; year-round transportation
- 🚴 **Bike infrastructure**: Boulder has dedicated bike lanes and the Boulder Creek Path
- 🔋 **E-bike option**: The modest elevation changes are perfect for e-bike assistance
- 🧴 **Post-ride**: Consider a quick stop at a downtown shower facility if available
- 🌤️ **Winter riding**: Use studded tires and bright lights for Boulder's snowy winters

| Factor | Detail |

## Alternative Scenic Route

For a more enjoyable ride, consider the **Boulder Creek Path**:
- Take the creek path south from near CU Boulder
- Follow the waterways west toward downtown
- Connect to Broadway via Canyon Boulevard
- Adds ~0.5 km but offers a scenic, car-free experience

## Bike Resources in Boulder

- **Boulder B-cycle** — Electric bike-share system with stations throughout the city
- **CU Bike Library** — Free or discounted bike rentals for CU students and staff
- **Boulder Bike Co-op** — Community bike shop for repairs and gear
