## Health Benefits Breakdown

### Walking
- **Cardiovascular:** ~400 kcal/trip strengthens heart and lungs
- **Mental health:** 48 min of outdoor activity reduces stress, anxiety, and depression symptoms
- **Weight management:** ~32,000 kcal burned per year (250 days × 400 kcal) = ~4.5 kg of fat equivalent
- **Bone density:** Weight-bearing exercise improves bone health, especially important at altitude
- **Sleep:** Regular physical activity improves sleep quality

### Biking
- **Cardiovascular:** ~200 kcal/trip with lower joint impact than walking
- **Muscle tone:** Engage glutes, quads, hamstrings, and core
- **Mental health:** 16 min of exercise + the joy of riding boosts mood
- **Longevity:** Studies show regular cyclists live 2–3 years longer on average
- **Air quality:** You breathe cleaner air on Canyon Blvd's separated path than in a car stuck on 19th Street

### Combined Benefits (3+ days/week)
- Reduce risk of heart disease by 30–40%
- Reduce risk of type 2 diabetes by 30%
- Improve cognitive function and productivity at work
- Lower healthcare costs long-term

## The Canyon Boulevard Bonus

The 637 m of separated bike lane on Canyon Boulevard is one of the best pieces of infrastructure in Boulder. Here's what makes it special:

- **Physically separated** from car traffic by a flexible bollard barrier
- **Shared-use path** — walkers, joggers, and cyclists all have dedicated space
- **Scenic route** — views of the Flatirons, Boulder Creek, wildlife (herons, turtles)
- **Flat terrain** — accessible to all fitness levels
- **Year-round** — the path is maintained even in winter (though ice can be an issue)

Riding this path even one trip per week makes Boulder's bike infrastructure more valued and more likely to be expanded.

## Methodology

- **Walking/Biking CO₂:** 0 kg (human-powered, no fuel)
- **Driving CO₂:** US EPA average of 0.404 kg CO₂/mile for a standard passenger vehicle → ~0.8 kg for 2.5 mi
- **B-Cycle CO₂:** ~0.005 kg per trip (small grid charge to recharge e-bikes, if applicable)
- **Calendar year basis:** 250 weekday commutes
- **Savings calculated:** Car baseline minus green mode minus B-Cycle (if used)
