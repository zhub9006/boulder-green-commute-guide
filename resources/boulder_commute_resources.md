# 🏙️ Boulder Commute Resources

## Bikes & Bike Infrastructure

- **BoulderBike.org** — Community hub for Boulder bike commuters
- **Bike Boulder** — Advocacy and education ([bikeboulder.org](https://bikeboulder.org))
- **Boulder B-Cycle** — Bike-sharing stations throughout downtown Boulder
  - Station at 11th & Broadway (near your office! 🎯)
  - Station at 13th & Pine
- **City of Boulder Bike Map** — Official bike route map
  - [bouldercolorado.gov/biketest](https://bouldercolorado.gov/biketest)
- **Bicycle Colorado** — Statewide advocacy for bike infrastructure

## Walking & Transit

- **Boulder Valley Comprehensive Transit Plan** — Regional transit planning
- **RTD Bus Routes** — Bus connections from Boulder to Denver and suburbs
  - Routes serving Broadway corridor: BX, AB1, AB2
- **SkyRide** — Seasonal ski bus routes
- **Boulder Walking Map** — Pedestrian-friendly route suggestions

## Local Neighborhoods Along the Route

- **Hill neighborhood** (near Oak Ave) — Walkable, close to CU Boulder
- **Downtown Boulder** — Destination for shopping, dining, and work
- **Uptown** — Residential area slightly west of route

## Weather & Planning Tools

- **Weather Underground** — Boulder-specific forecasts
- **Caltopo** — Topographic map of Boulder for route planning
- **AllTrails** — Walking and hiking trail info for weekend adventures
- **Boulder Webcams** — Live views of conditions: [bouldercolorado.gov/webcam](https://www.bouldercolorado.gov/webcam)

## Environment & Sustainability

- **Boulder Climate Action Plan** — City-wide sustainability initiative
  - [bouldercolorado.gov/climate](https://bouldercolorado.gov/climate)
- **Travel Options** — Commute alternatives program: [traveloptions.org](https://traveloptions.org)
- **Boulder Creek Days** — Annual celebration of Boulder's waterways

## Emergency & Safety

- **Boulder Police Non-Emergency:** 303-441-3333
- **Emergency:** 911
- **Boulder Community Hospital:** 1100 Balsam Ave (short ride from route)

## Getting Started Checklist

1. **Test the route on a weekend** ✅ Walk or bike it on a day off first
2. **Note the sunset time**🌅 Winter evenings get dark early on 19th Street
3. **Pack smart**🎒 Small backpack with water, layers, and rain gear
4. **Connect with others**🤝 Look for fellow green commuters on your route!
5. **Track your progress**📊 Use Strava or the Boulder commutes dashboard
6. **Check bike maintenance**🔧 ABCs: Air, Brakes, Chain
