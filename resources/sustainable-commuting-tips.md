# Sustainable Commuting Tips for Boulder Residents

## Getting Started

### Start Small
Begin by trying walking or biking 1-2 days per week and gradually increase. Even one car-free day per week makes a difference.

### Check the Weather
Boulder weather can change rapidly. Check forecasts before heading out and dress in layers.

### Plan Your Route
Use this guide and OpenStreetMap-based routing apps to find the safest, most efficient routes.

---

## Walking Tips

1. **Invest in good shoes** — Proper footwear makes all the difference on longer walks
2. **Carry a daypack** — Keep essentials (water, layers, phone) with you
3. **Use pedestrian infrastructure** — Stay on sidewalks and use crosswalks
4. **Be visible** — Wear bright colors, especially in low-light conditions
5. **Enjoy the journey** — Walking lets you notice Boulder's beautiful neighborhoods

---

## Biking Tips

1. **Get the right bike** — A comfortable bike suited to your needs is key
2. **Safety first** — Always wear a helmet and use lights at night
3. **Use bike lanes** — Boulder has an expanding network of bike infrastructure
4. **Lock it up** — Use secure bike parking, especially downtown
5. **Maintain your bike** — Keep tires inflated and brakes adjusted

---

## Combining Modes

### Bike + Bus
- RTD bikeways program allows bikes on most buses
- Great for days with bad weather or longer distances
- Check [RTD's bike policy](https://www.rtd-denver.com/bikes) for details

### Walk + Transit
- Many RTD stops are walkable from north Boulder neighborhoods
- Combine walking with bus for longer commutes

## Environmental Impact

### By the Numbers
- **Average car commute in Boulder:** ~16 km round trip
- **CO₂ per car trip:** ~3.5 kg
- **Annual CO₂ from commuting 5 days/week:** ~840 kg

### What You Can Save

| Mode Change | CO₂ Saved/Year | Equivalent |
|-------------|----------------|------------|
| Drive → Bike 2 days/week | ~336 kg | 85 tree seedlings grown for 10 years |
| Drive → Walk 2 days/week | ~336 kg | 85 tree seedlings grown for 10 years |
| Drive → Bike 5 days/week | ~840 kg | 212 tree seedlings grown for 10 years |
