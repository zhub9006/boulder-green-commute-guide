# 📍 Boulder Transportation & Green Resources

## Public Transit

### RTD (Regional Transportation District)
- **Website:** [rtd-denver.com](https://www.rtd-denver.com/)
- **Serves:** Boulder, Denver metro, and surrounding areas
- **Bus Routes:** Multiple routes through downtown Boulder
- **Rail:** Flatiron Flyer (FF1) express bus from Boulder to Denver
- **Fares:** $2.60 single ride; $5.00 day pass; $100/month unlimited
- **Bike on Bus:** All RTD buses have bike racks (front-mounted, 2 bikes)

### Boulder Metro Transit
- **Local bus routes** connecting Boulder neighborhoods
- **Free Metro Transit** for Boulder residents (with student ID)

## Bike Resources

### Boulder BCycle
- **Website:** [gobouldercycles.com](https://www.gobouldercycles.com/)
- **Locations:** 30+ stations across Boulder
- **Pricing:** $15/month for unlimited 30-min rides; $85/year
- **Great for:** Trying biking without owning a bike, or short errands

### Bicycle Colorado
- **Website:** [bicyclecolorado.org](https://bicyclecolorado.org/)
- **Advocacy:** Statewide advocacy for better bike infrastructure
- **Resources:** Safe cycling tips, route guides, community rides

### City of Boulder Bicycle Program
- **Website:** [bouldercolorado.gov/transportation/bicycling](https://www.bouldercolorado.gov/transportation/bicycling)
- **Programs:** Bike lane improvements, Traffic calming, Bicycle/pedestrian counts, Safe Routes to School

## Car-Free Alternatives

### RTD Bus + Bike
- Use RTD buses with your bike for longer distances
- **Flatiron Flyer (FF1):** Boulder → Denver in ~55 min
- **J Route:** Connects Boulder to Denver via Golden, Lakewood

### Car Sharing
- **Zipcar:** Available at several Boulder locations
- **Turo:** Peer-to-peer car rental

### Electric Scooters
- **Lime:** Available around downtown Boulder
- **Spin:** Limited availability

## Walking Resources

### Boulder Walk Score
- **Boulder overall:** 73/100 (Very Walkable)
- **Downtown Boulder:** 85/100
- **Pearl Street area:** 90/100
- **Resource:** [walkscore.com](https://www.walkscore.com/)

### Pedestrian Safety
- **Crosswalks:** Every intersection has a crosswalk (Colorado law)
- **Right of way:** Pedestrians have the right of way at marked crosswalks
