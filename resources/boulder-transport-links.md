# Boulder Sustainable Transport Resources

## Official City Resources

- **[Boulder Transportation Page](https://bouldercolorado.gov/transportation)** — City info on bike lanes, sidewalks, transit
- **[Boulder Bike Paths Map](https://bouldercolorado.gov/healthrecreation/bike-paths)** — Interactive map of all bike facilities
- **[Boulder Climate Action Plan](https://bouldercolorado.gov/climate-action-plan)** — City's net-zero by 2035 roadmap

## Transit

- **[RTD Denver Regional Transit](https://rtd-denver.com/)** — Regional bus and light rail
  - Route **101** (Boulder Valley Corridor) — serves Broadway corridor
  - Route **102** — additional local service
  - **SB1** (Spark/BBARC) — regional bus rapid transit on US-36
- **[RTD Trip Planner](https://rtd-denver.com/ridemap)** — Plan your transit trip

## Bike Infrastructure & Advocacy

- **[Bicycle Info Project](https://bicycleinfoproject.com/)** — National bike safety info
- **[PeopleForBikes](https://peopleforbikes.org/)** — National bike advocacy with Boulder chapter
- **[Boulder Cycling Club](https://bouldercyclingclub.org/)** — Local cycling community, events, and group rides
- **[B-Cycle Boulder Bike Share](https://www.bcycle.com/boulder-co/)** — Bike-share stations across Boulder

## Walking & Pedestrian Resources

- **[Walk Boulder](https://www.walkboulder.org/)** — Pedestrian advocacy group
- **[Boulder Creek Path](https://bouldercolorado.gov/healthrecreation/parks-trails-exhibits/boulder-creek-path)** — Dedicated pedestrian/cyclist path along the creek
- **[OpenStreetMap Boulder](https://www.openstreetmap.org/#map=14/40.0150/-105.2700)** — Contribute to Boulder's mapping data

## Electric & Micromobility

- **[Boulder EV Charging Stations](https://bouldercolorado.gov/electric_vehicle_charging_stations)** — EV infrastructure locations
- **[Lime / Bird / Spin](https://www.becycle.info/)** — E-scooter and e-bike sharing options in Boulder

## Route Planning Tools

- **[Google Maps (Biking)](https://www.google.com/maps/dir//boulder+co/biking)** — Google's biking directions
- **[Komoot](https://www.komoot.com/)** — Outdoor route planning with elevation profiles
- **[Strava](https://www.strava.com/)** — Fitness tracking and route discovery
- **[OpenRouteService](https://maps.openrouteservice.org/)** — Open-source routing alternative
