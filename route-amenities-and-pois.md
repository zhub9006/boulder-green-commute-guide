## 🗺️ How to Use This Guide

### During Your Morning Commute
1. **Grab coffee** at Capital One Cafe or Ku Cha House of Tea on Pearl Street before heading through the mall
2. **Fuel up** at Wahoo's Fish Taco if you're running late
3. **Quick errand** — swing by the grocery or pharmacy on Pearl Street on your way

### During Your Evening Commute
1. **Dinner plans** — Jax Fish House or Pasta Jay's are steps from your office on Pearl Street
2. **Bike repair** — University Bicycles is open until 6PM Mon–Sat for any post-commute issues
3. **Social stop** — Grab ice cream at Ben & Jerry's or a tea at Ku Cha House

### Emergency & Support
- **Bike tire flat?** University Bicycles (Mon–Sat 10AM–6PM) or carry a CO2 inflator
- **Phone dead?** Capital One Cafe has outlets and free Wi-Fi
- **First aid?** Check your office building first; Ideal Dermatology is nearby during business hours
