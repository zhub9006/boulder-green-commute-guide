# Walking Route Details

**Route**: 45 Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway, Boulder
**Total Distance**: 4.03 km (2.51 miles)
**Waypoints**: Oak Avenue (start) → Canyon Boulevard (end)

## Turn-by-Turn Directions

### Leg 1: Oak Avenue
- **Distance**: 97 meters
- **Duration**: ~16 seconds
- **Description**: Begin at 45 Oak Avenue heading south on Oak Avenue.

### Leg 2: 19th Street
- **Distance**: 2.16 km (1.34 miles)
- **Duration**: ~3.5 minutes
- **Description**: Turn onto 19th Street and head south. This is the longest segment of the route, running along the east side of the CU Boulder campus area.

### Leg 3: 20th Street (Short Connector)
- **Distance**: 106 meters
- **Duration**: ~12 seconds
- **Description**: Brief connector onto 20th Street.

### Leg 4: 20th Street (Continued)
- **Distance**: 566 meters
- **Duration**: ~1 minute
- **Description**: Continue along 20th Street heading south.

### Leg 5: Pearl Street
- **Distance**: 216 meters
- **Duration**: ~26 seconds
- **Description**: Turn onto Pearl Street, Boulder's vibrant downtown pedestrian corridor.

### Leg 6: 18th Street
- **Distance**: 249 meters
- **Duration**: ~37 seconds
- **Description**: Turn onto 18th Street heading south.

### Leg 7: Canyon Boulevard (Final Approach)
- **Distance**: 637 meters
- **Duration**: ~59 seconds
- **Description**: Turn onto Canyon Boulevard and continue to your destination at 1800 Broadway.

## Walking Tips

- ✅ **Best for**: Leisurely commute, scenic downtown exploration, zero-emission travel
- ⚠️ **Consider**: Boulder's altitude (5,430 ft) may feel tiring on inclines
- 🌤️ **Weather**: Summer monsoon season — carry water and a rain jacket
- 🚦 **Crossings**: Pay attention to traffic signals on Pearl Street and Canyon Boulevard

## Nearby Amenities Along the Route

- Coffee shops on Pearl Street
- Restaurants on 19th and 20th Streets
- CU Boulder campus landmarks
- Boulder Creek Path (parallel route option)
