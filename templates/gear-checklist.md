# 🎒 Gear Checklist for Green Commuting

## Essential Gear (Start Here)

### For Walking
- [ ] Comfortable, supportive walking shoes
- [ ] Weather-appropriate clothing (layers)
- [ ] Small daypack or crossbody bag
- [ ] Reusable water bottle
- [ ] Light rain jacket (packable)
- [ ] Sunscreen (summer)
- [ ] Sunglasses

### For Biking
- [ ] Reliable bicycle (well-maintained; tuned up at least twice/year)
- [ ] **U-lock** (frame + wheel to fixed object) — most important security item
- [ ] Front and rear lights (even for daytime visibility)
- [ ] Helmet (legally required in Boulder, and it saves lives)
- [ ] Weather-appropriate cycling clothing
- [ ] Fenders (keeps road spray off your clothes)
- [ ] Hand pump or CO₂ inflator
- [ ] Spare inner tube
- [ ] Small seat bag or saddlebag with essentials

---

## Nice-to-Have Gear

- [ ] Bike-specific shoes (clip-in or comfortable flat pedals)
- [ ] Cycling gloves for grip and comfort
- [ ] Reflective vest or ankle bands for visibility
- [ ] Phone mount for navigation
- [ ] Waterproof panniers or backpack
- [ ] Sunglasses with mounts (prevents bouncing)
- [ ] Audible bell (required by law in Boulder)
- [ ] GoPro or phone mount for documenting your commute
- [ ] Small luggage rack (for carrying work items)

---

## Rain Commute Kit

- [ ] Waterproof jacket ( Gore-Tex or similar)
- [ ] Waterproof pants (over-leg type)
- [ ] Waterproof shoe covers or boots
- [ ] Waterproof handlebar bag or pannier
- [ ] Clear rain goggles (optional but helpful)
- [ ] Extra layer (because you'll get cold faster when wet)

## Winter Commute Kit

- [ ] Insulated, waterproof boots
- [ ] Thermal base layers (top and bottom)
- [ ] Windproof mid-layer
- [ ] Insulated, waterproof outer shell
- [ ] Balaclava or neck gaiter
- [ ] Insulated, waterproof gloves (not just knit)
- [ ] Studded tires (for icy paths)
- [ ] Hand warmers (disposable or reusable)
- [ ] High-vis reflective gear (winter light = short days)

---

## Pre-Departure Quick Checklist

- [ ] Bike tires inflated to proper PSI (check weekly)
- [ ] Brakes working
- [ ] Lights charged/installed
- [ ] U-lock and keys accessible
- [ ] Weather checked for the day
- [ ] Work items packed and ready
- [ ] Backups: spare tube, mini-pump, phone charged
