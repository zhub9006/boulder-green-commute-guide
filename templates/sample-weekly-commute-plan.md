# 📅 Sample Weekly Commute Plan

A practical guide to gradually integrating walking and biking into your weekly routine.

---

## Week 1: Dip Your Toes

| Day | Mode | Minutes | Notes |
|-----|------|---------|-------|
| Mon | 🚗 Drive (regular) | 10 | Start as normal |
| Tue | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Experience the route at your own pace |
| Wed | 🚗 Drive (regular) | 10 | Rest day |
| Thu | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Build the habit |
| Fri | 🚗 Drive (regular) | 10 | End-of-week treat |

**Goal:** Get comfortable with the walking route twice this week.

---

## Week 2: Introduce Biking

| Day | Mode | Minutes | Notes |
|-----|------|---------|-------|
| Mon | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Continue walking routine |
| Tue | 🚲 Bike | 20 | Try the bike route this week |
| Wed | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Mix it up |
| Thu | 🚲 Bike | 20 | Build biking confidence |
| Fri | 🚗 Drive (regular) | 10 | End-of-week treat |

**Goal:** Establish biking as a regular option.

---

## Week 3: Find Your Rhythm

| Day | Mode | Minutes | Notes |
|-----|------|---------|-------|
| Mon | 🚲 Bike | 20 | Start with the faster option |
| Tue | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Active recovery morning |
| Wed | 🚲 Bike | 20 | Quick and efficient |
| Thu | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Take your time, enjoy the creek path |
| Fri | 🚗 Drive (regular) | 10 | or 🚲 Bike if feeling energetic |

**Goal:** Alternate by preference and weather.

---

## Week 4: Make It Yours

| Day | Mode | Minutes | Notes |
|-----|------|---------|-------|
| Mon | 🚲 Bike | 20 | Your new normal |
| Tue | 🚶 Walk | 50 | When weather is nice, walk! |
| Wed | 🚶 Walk | 50 | Active, refreshing mid-week |
| Thu | 🚲 Bike | 20 | Fast and reliable |
| Fri | 🚶/🚲 | 20–50 | Flexible — choose by mood or weather |

**Goal:** 3–4 green commutes per week is a sustainable balance for most people.

---

## Progression Tips

- **Don't overthink it** — Even one green commute per week makes a difference
- **Weather never stops you** — With the right gear, all-weather commuting is doable
  - Rain: Waterproof jacket + pants, fenders, lights
  - Cold: Layered clothing, balaclava, insulated gloves
  - Wind: Windbreaker, tighter routes
- **Flexibility is key** — Walk one way, drive the other on tough days
- **Celebrate milestones** — Completed 10 bike commutes? Treat yourself!

---

## The "Always Have an Out" Principle

- Keep your car keys accessible for emergency drives
- Know the RTD bus schedule as a backup
- A rideshare app can be a last-resort safety net
- **The worst that happens is you drive — you haven't lost anything!**

---

*This is just a template. Adjust based on your schedule, fitness level, and weather!*
