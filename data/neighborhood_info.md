# 🏘️ Boulder Neighborhood Commuter Profile

## Route: Oak Avenue → 1800 Broadway

### Area Characteristics

**Origin Area (Oak Avenue / 80304):**
- Historic residential neighborhood
- Walk Score: ~85 (Very Walkable)
- Bike Score: ~80 (Very Bikeable)
- Transit Score: ~65 (Good Transit)
- Nearby amenities: Grocery stores, cafes, parks, library

**Destination Area (Broadway / 80302):**
- Downtown Boulder / CU campus area
- Walk Score: ~95 (Walker's Paradise)
- Bike Score: ~90 (Biker's Paradise)
- Transit Score: ~80 (Excellent Transit)
- Nearby amenities: Restaurants, shops, university, medical center

### Terrain Analysis
- **Elevation change:** Minimal — Boulder is famously flat in the base area
- **Grade:** Nearly flat (< 2% grade on this route)
- **Surface:** Asphalt roads + brick pavers on Pearl Street + crushed gravel on Canyon Blvd path
- **Exposure:** Canyon Boulevard segment is shaded by cottonwoods

### Traffic Patterns
- **Morning rush (7-9 AM):** 19th Street can be busy; use bike lanes
- **Midday:** Light traffic, great biking
- **Evening (4-6 PM):** Heavy traffic; consider RTD bus if weather is bad
- **Weekends:** Minimal traffic; scenic route recommended
