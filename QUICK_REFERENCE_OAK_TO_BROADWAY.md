## Key Tips

### 🚶 Walking
- Use sidewalks and crosswalks — especially on 19th St.
- The route gently slopes downhill — enjoy the ride!
- Layer up: Boulder weather shifts quickly.
- Downtown is busy 7–9 AM and 4–6 PM.

### 🚴 Biking
- **Canyon Boulevard** has a protected bike path — use it!
- 19th St. is the most exposed segment — stay alert.
- Dismount in Pearl Street pedestrian zones if preferred.
- Carry lights (CO law requires them at night) and a U-lock.
- Wind gusts on Canyon Blvd can be significant.
