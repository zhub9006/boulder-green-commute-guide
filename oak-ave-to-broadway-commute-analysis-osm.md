## 🗓️ Seasonal Recommendations

### Spring–Fall (March–November) ✅ Ideal
- Perfect weather for both walking and biking
- Long daylight hours for flexible scheduling
- Bring layers for temperature variation
- Watch for afternoon thunderstorms (June–August)

### Winter (December–February) ❄️
- **Walking:** Still very feasible with proper boots and layers
- **Biking:** Requires winter prep — studded/fat tires, fenders, lights, waterproof gear
- Most dedicated bike lanes are plowed promptly
- Consider combining with RTD bus on worst weather days
