# Route Comparison: Walking vs. Biking — July 2026

## Same Route, Two Experiences

Both modes cover the same 4.03 km / 2.51-mile path from **45 Oak Avenue** to **1800 Broadway**, Boulder, CO. The choice between them depends on your time, weather, fitness goals, and how you want to experience Boulder.

## When to Walk

- ✅ You want exercise as part of your commute
- ✅ Weather is mild and dry
- ✅ You have 50+ minutes in your morning routine
- ✅ You enjoy the quiet residential streets and small-town feel
- ✅ You're carrying light items (no heavy backpack needed)
- ✅ You want zero infrastructure cost

## When to Bike

- ✅ You want to save time (~16 min vs ~50 min)
- ✅ You're running late or have a tight schedule
- ✅ Weather is windy or cool (biking generates more heat)
- ✅ You want to cover more distance without sweating excessively
- ✅ You're commuting year-round and want consistency
- ✅ You want to arrive fresh and ready for work

## When to Consider E-Bike

- ✅ You want the speed of biking with less effort
- ✅ The gentle southward grade from Oak Ave to Broadway is almost flat
- ✅ You want to arrive without sweating
- ✅ You plan to bike even in slightly challenging conditions
- ✅ Annual cost is reasonable ($100–200 vs $500–1,200 driving)

## Their Route

Both modes follow the same sequence of streets:

1. **Oak Avenue** (97 m) — residential, gentle start
2. **19th Street** (2.16 km) — longest segment, southbound through neighborhood
3. **20th St** (106 m) — brief transition
4. **20th Street** (566 m) — continue south, wider road
5. **Pearl Street** (216 m) — turn right, iconic Boulder street
6. **18th Street** (249 m) — short stretch through downtown
7. **Canyon Boulevard** (637 m) — finish along Canyon Blvd to office

## Boulder-Specific Advantages

Boulder is one of the best bike-friendly cities in Colorado. This route benefits from:

- 🚲 **200+ miles of paved bike paths** throughout the city
- 🚶 **Extensive pedestrian network** with well-marked crosswalks
- 🌲 **Flat terrain** on this route — minimal elevation gain
- 🏙️ **Walkable downtown** — Pearl Street Mall is pedestrian-only
- 🔒 **Low crime** — Boulder consistently ranks among safest mid-sized cities
- 🚌 **B-Cycle stations** near both endpoints for hybrid commutes
