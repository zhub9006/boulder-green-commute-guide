4. **🎧 Entertainment:** 50 min walk = 3–4 podcast episodes; 16 min bike = 1 long-form episode
5. **👥 Buddy system:** Find a walking/biking partner — safer and more fun

7. **🚲 B-Cycle backup:** The B-Cycle station near 1800 Broadway is there if your phone dies or you're running late
8. **⏰ Timing:** Avoid the 4:30–5:30 PM window on 19th Street when student traffic peaks
