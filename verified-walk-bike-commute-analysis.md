## 💡 Pro Tips

### For Walkers
1. **Earbuds & podcasts** — The 19th Street stretch is long and monotonous; load up a podcast
2. **Layer up** — Boulder weather changes quickly, especially in shoulder seasons
3. **Use the Foothills Trail** — In summer, consider cutting through the CU Boulder campus paths for a more scenic route
4. **Carry a backpack** — Keep a change of shoes at the office

### For Bikers
1. **Canyon Boulevard bike lane** — This is your safest and most pleasant segment; stay in the bike lane
2. **B-Cycle stations** — Several stations along this route if you want a one-way e-bike trip
3. **Fenders are essential** — Boulder's spring mud and winter slush require them
4. **Bike parking at 1800 Broadway** — Downtown Boulder has ample bike racks; lock up securely
