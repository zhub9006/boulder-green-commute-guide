💡 Quick Tips for Boulder

🏔️ Altitude: Boulder is at 5,430 ft — take it easier on the first few rides and hydrate well.
🌦️ Weather: Afternoon thunderstorms are common July–August. Carry a rain jacket.
☀️ Sun: UV is ~25% stronger at altitude. Sunscreen and sunglasses are a must.
🚲 B-cycle: $12/month for unlimited rides. Reserve bikes via the app!
👥 Group: Join the Boulder Commuter Club for challenges and rewards.
